Funeral services for Gayla Christine Hudson, will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, March 3, 2009, at the Hopeton Wesleyan Church with Rev. Marion Hadsell and Rev. Dave Keller officiating.  Interment will be in the Pleasant View Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Gayla Christine Hudson, daughter of Carroll Dean and Beverly Gail (Nusser) Eckels, was born June 14, 1963, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away March 1, 2009, at Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, at the age of 45 years, 8 months, and 17 days.  
Gayla graduated from Carmen High School and attended NWOSU for two years.  She was also a certified dietary manager and worked as such at the Homestead in Alva and Crystal Wood in Woodward.  She was employed as a convenience store manager at Camp Houston Country Store at the time of her death.  
She enjoyed reading, crossword puzzles, and being with her grandchildren.  
Gayla is survived by two daughters, Angela Murrow and fianc?, Michael Lee, of Macksville, Kansas, and Lezlie Eckels of Macksville, Kansas; her parents, Dean and Beverly Eckels of Carmen; her companion, Darrell Myers and his daughter, Daralene Myers, of Mooreland; one brother, Jerry Eckels and wife, Lisa, of Stillwater, Oklahoma; and four grandchildren, Billy Lee, James Lee, Danielle Binns, and Devin McMurphy; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home for an educational fund for her children.
