Doris Mae Parsons, daughter of the late Francis Leonard and Gladys Mercie (Cox) Armstrong, was born September 22, 1922, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away May 19, 2008, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 85 years, 7 months, and 26 days.  
Doris spent her childhood in Alva and graduated from Alva High School.  On December 23, 1970, she was united in marriage to Ben Parsons at Enid, Oklahoma.  They made their home in Enid until he passed away, at which time, she moved to Alva.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Ben; and one son, Leonard Burling.  
Doris is survived by one daughter, Karen Caldwell and her husband, Vaughn, of Alva; two sisters, Frances Jean Foster of Wichita; and Donna Lee Schwerdtfeger and her husband, Orlie, of Alva; four grandchildren, Kelly Caldwell of Alva, Kimberly Caldwell and her fianc??, Larry Burger, of Bend, Oregon, Timothy Caldwell and his wife, Teresa of Owasso, Oklahoma; and Sloan Burling and his wife, Melissa, of Mooreland, Oklahoma; six great grandchildren; one great great grandchild; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Share Convalescent Center Activity Fund.  
Doris was cremated at her request and there will be a memorial service at a later date.  Arrangement were under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.
