Funeral services for Eddie Leon Bell will be 2:00 p.m. Saturday, December 19, 2009, at the First Baptist Church with Rev. David Shaw officiating. Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Eddie Leon Bell, son of the late Elvie Allison and Lorraine (Peppers) Bell Baldridge, was born June 27, 1947, at Coweta, Oklahoma, and passed away December 16, 2009, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 62 years, 5 months, and 19 days.  
Eddie attended Waynoka School, then entered the United States Army where he received his GED. On March 21, 1978, he was united in marriage to Terri Lynn Chandler at Tulsa, Oklahoma. He worked as a truck driver for a time before beginning his career in law enforcement. He started his career in the Waynoka Police Department, then worked in Oilton, Creek County, and Sperry before his retirement.  
He was a member of the Metro Faith Christian Church in Tulsa. He enjoyed fishing and woodworking, but his favorite pastime was spending time with his children and grandchildren.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by one brother, Teddy Bell; and two sisters, Jonnie Williams and Pat Triplett.   
Eddie is survived by his wife, Terri, of Waynoka; one son, Eddie Bell, Jr. of Tulsa; three daughters, Kathy Bell and Jose Castillo of Tulsa, Ellie Waggoner of Tulsa, and Janiece Bell and Cody Warfield of Waynoka; one sister, Janie Cunningham and husband, Ivan, of Waynoka; one brother, Buck Phillips and wife, Tana, of Ensign, Kansas; six grandchildren; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
