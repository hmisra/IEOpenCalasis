Funeral services for Patricia Ann Triplitt will be 3:00 p.m. Friday, April 4, 2008, at the First Baptist Church with Rev. David Shaw officiating.  Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Patricia Ann Triplitt, daughter of the late Elvie Allison and Dorothy Lorraine (Peppers) Bell, was born February 18, 1945, at Coweta, Oklahoma, and passed away March 31, 2008, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 63 years, 1 month, and 15 days.  
Pat attended school at Inola and Waynoka before receiving her Licensed Practical Nurse degree at Drumright, Oklahoma.  She worked as an LPN in several places, including the Waynoka Nursing Home, a VA hospital in Colorado, in Enid and in Home Health.  On March 30, 1990, she was united in marriage to Kenneth Triplitt in Woodward.  
She was a member of the Waynoka Assembly of God Church.  She enjoyed sewing and quilting, but she really adored her grandchildren and great grandchildren.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Kenneth; one brother, Ted Bell; and one sister, Jonnie Williams.  
Pat is survived by four sons, Robert Bence, Jr. and wife, Diana, of Manhattan, Kansas, Kenneth Bence and wife, LouAnn, of Woodward, Scott Bence and wife, Nikki, of Big Piney, Wyoming, and Larry Goff and wife, Michele of Tulsa; three daughters, Rebecca Cope and Paul Nutter of Waynoka, Robin Bence of Miami, Florida, and Kimberly Hall of Coos Bay, Oregon; one brother, Eddie Bell and wife, Terri, of Waynoka; one sister Janie Cunningham and husband, Ivan, of Waynoka; 23 grandchildren, several great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka EMTs.
