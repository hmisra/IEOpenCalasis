Mass of Christian Burial for Lupe B. Negrete will be 1:30 p.m. Saturday, July 3, 2010, at Sacred Heart Catholic Church in Alva with Father Damian Lawrence officiating.  Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka LLC.  
Lupe B. Negrete, daughter of the late Tiburcio Torres and Rosalia (Aguirre) Negrete, was born February 3, 1929, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away June 24, 2010, at Bryan, Texas, at the age of 81 years, 4 months, and 21 days.  
Lupe attended Waynoka Schools.  She lived all of her life in Waynoka where she worked at the Waynoka Nursing Home over 30 years.  She was a hard working lady who also cleaned houses for several people over the years.  
Lupe was a very active member of Our Mother of Mercy Church in Waynoka, spending a lot of time taking care of the church, watering the plants and cleaning.  She enjoyed walking and playing dominoes.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by one brother Esabel "Easy" Negrete; two sisters, Consuela Negrete, and Elvira "Berta? Martinez; and an infant brother.  
Lupe is survived by one brother, Jess Negrete of Chicago, Illinois; one sister, Rose Marie Sifuentes of Hearne, Texas; many nieces and nephews; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Our Mother of Mercy Church.
