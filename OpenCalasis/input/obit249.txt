Mass of Christian Burial for Deborah Denise Estes will be 10:00 a.m. Monday, August 26, 2013, at Sacred Heart Catholic Church with Father James Wickersham serving as the celebrant.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Deborah Denise Estes, daughter of the late Dean and Connie (Simpkins) Smith, was born January 16, 1966 in San Angelo, Texas, and passed away August 17, 2013, in Santa Fe, New Mexico, at the age of 47 years, 7 months, and 1 day.    
She loved to ride bikes with her friends and enjoyed the outdoors.    
Deborah was preceeded in death by her parents, Dean and Connie Smith; a daughter, Desiree Smith; her aunt, Fleta Dawn Frye; her grandparents, Wibur Simpkins and Fleta McMurphy; and her cousin, Jayme Diefenbach.    
Deborah is survived by her children, Constance Lear Mitchell and husband, Cory, of Casper, Wyoming, Marie Smith, Dale Smith, and Tina Renee Smith; her brother, Dennis Smith of Texas, her boyfriend, Jerry Pearson of Santa Fe; her aunt and uncle, Billy and Janet Smith of Hardtner; an uncle, Larry James of Shawnee; and family who loves her.    
  Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Sacred Heart Church.
