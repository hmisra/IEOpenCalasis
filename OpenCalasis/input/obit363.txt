Graveside services for Marvin Rudolph Parsons will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, January 9, 2013, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Rev. John Smith officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.    
Marvin Rudolph Parsons, son of the late Dennis and Margaret (Hada) Parsons, was born May 24, 1928, at Freedom, Oklahoma, and passed away January 7, 2013, at Woodward, Oklahoma, at the age of 84 years, 7 months, and 14 days.    
Marvin graduated Waynoka High School with the Class of 1946.  He spent the majority of his life living in the Woodward area where he worked for the Santa Fe Railroad for 35 years.  He then served as a bus driver for the Woodward Schools until he retired in 1988.    
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by three brothers, Herb, Ralph, and Norman; and a stepson, Mike.    
Marvin is survived by a daughter, Marva Jo; other relatives and friends.    
The family would like to thank Grace Living Center for taking such good care of Marvin over the last 13 years.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Grace Living Center.    
Remembrances may be shared with the family at www.marshallfuneralhomes.com.
