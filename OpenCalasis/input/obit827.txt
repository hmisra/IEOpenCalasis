Funeral services for Patricia Lynn (Johnson) Hanes will be 2:00 p.m. Saturday, January 3, 2015, at the Waynoka Church of God with Reverend Woody Hamon officiating.  Interment will be in the Galena Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.    
Patricia Lynn (Johnson) Hanes, daughter of Dennis Lynn and Lola Mae (Hejduk) Johnson, was born December 24, 1974, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away December 29, 2014, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 40 years and 5 days.    
Patricia graduated from Waynoka High School.  On December 6, 1992, she was united in marriage to James Willard Hanes, Jr. at Waynoka, Oklahoma.  They lived in Waynoka where they operated Jim's Sand Toys for 15 years.  They moved to Pratt, Kansas where she worked at an electric company. She returned to Waynoka about 2 years ago.    
Patricia attended the Assembly of God Church.  She loved riding the dunes on four wheelers and participating in the sand drag races.  She also enjoyed scrap booking, riding bicycles, and fishing with her family.    
Patricia is survived by one son, Brant Ward Hanes of Pratt, Kansas; one daughter, Tara Lynn Hanes of Mountainview, Oklahoma;  parents, Dennis and Lola Johnson of Waynoka; and two brothers, Bruce Anthony Johnson and wife, Teresa, of Enid, and Keith Wayne Johnson of Waynoka; one grandson, Anakin Hanes; other relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Share Hospice or the Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for cancer research.
