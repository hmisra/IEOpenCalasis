Graveside services for Norma Gene Allen will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, February 16, 2009, at the Alva Municipal Cemetery with Rev. Mark Bersche, pastor of the Zion Lutheran Church officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  There will be a visitation at Marshall Funeral Home from 6:00 p.m. to 8:00 p.m. on Monday, February 15.  
Norma Gene Allen, daughter of the late Nelson and Alice (Nelson) Pennington, was born August 13, 1925, in Woods County, Oklahoma, and passed away February 12, 2010, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 84 years, 5 months, and 30 days.  
Norma Gene attended Union School and Dacoma High School before graduating from Alva High School with the Class of 1943.  She lived all of her life in Alva where she worked 35 years for the Morford and Benson Law Firm as a legal secretary, retiring in 1991.  
She was a member of the Zion Lutheran Church and the Beta Sigma Phi Sorority.  She enjoyed playing cards, flower gardening and loved animals, especially cats.  
Norma Gene is survived by two sons, Nelson Allen and wife, Beverly, of Enid, and Douglas Allen and wife, Joann, of Midlothian, Texas; four grandchildren, Robert Allen and wife, Maggie, of Chicago, Illinois, Tara Ruiz and husband, Cris, of Sealy, Texas, John Allen and wife, Brandi, of Pampa, Texas, and James Allen and wife Debbie, of Cedar Park, Texas; eight great grandchildren, Seth and Morgan Allen, Erik and Derik Ruiz, Patrick, Paige, and Chloe Allen, and Trysten Plunk; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Creature Concerns.
