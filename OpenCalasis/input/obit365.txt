Graveside services for Murl Allen Knapp will be 11:00 a.m. Saturday, December 11, 2010, at the Square Cedar Cemetery with Rev. David Shaw officiating. Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Murl Allen Knapp, son of the late William Logan and Marjorie Pauline (Coontz) Knapp, was born May 6, 1961, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away December 7, 2010, north of Waynoka, at the age of 49 years, 7 months, and 1 day.  
Murl lived in Waynoka all of his life and graduated from Waynoka High School with the Class of 1980. He worked at several jobs during his life, always doing whatever he could. Lately, he was working with his brother and sister to recycle scrap iron.  
Murl is survived by three brothers, Mac Knapp and wife, Shahla, of Albuquerque, New Mexico, David Knapp of Waynoka, and Dennis Knapp of Sallisaw; one sister, Joleta Tidwell of Waynoka; two nephews, Scott Knapp and Brian Tidwell; one niece, Amber Tidwell; other relatives and many friends.  
In lieu of flowers, the family requests that memorial contributions be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
