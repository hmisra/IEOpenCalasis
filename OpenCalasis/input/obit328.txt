Graveside services for Dolores Maxine Zook will be 1:00 p.m. Monday, July 1, 2013, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Pastor Ken Higgins officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.    
Dolores Maxine Zook, daughter of the late Lawrence and Helen Maxine (Cox) Brewer, was born October 18, 1946, at Alva, and passed away June 24, 2013, at her home in Waynoka, at the age of 66 years, 8 months, and 6 days.    
Dolores attended Alva Schools.  She worked as a sales clerk at Walmart for many years.    
She was a member of the Waynoka Church of God.  She enjoyed crafts.    
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her daughter, Glenda Gibby; and two siblings.    
Dolores is survived by three sons, David Hawkins and wife, Jenni, of Waynoka, William Hawkins of Waynoka, and Les Bedwell of Ardmore; two sisters, Nancy Woodard of Waynoka and Roberta Curtis of Salt Lake City, UT; 14 grandchildren; 11 nieces and nephews; other relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.    
Remembrances may be shared with the family at www.marshallfuneralhomes.com.
