Graveside services for Allen Keith Maul will be 2:00 p.m. Monday, April 12, 2010, at the Garrison Cemetery with Reverend Connie Kinnard officiating.  Interment will be  under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC. 	 Allen Keith Maul was born to Carol Joyce Lindsley on March 5, 1963, at Denver, Colorado, and passed away suddenly on April 7, 2010, at his residence in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, at the age of 47 years, 1 month, and 2 days. 	 Allen grew up and attended school in Colorado before moving with his family to Dacoma and attending the Carmen- Dacoma Schools. He married Crystal  Parker and later became engaged to Cynthia Hayes. 	 He enjoyed fishing, working on cars, traveling, computers, and he had a fascination with eagles. 	 Allen was preceded in death by his mother. 	 Surviving are his father and stepmother, Wayne and Sharon Maul, of Cleo Springs; his fiance?, Cynthia Hayes, of Oklahoma City; his children, Levi Morris of Missouri, Dusti Maul, Chelsea Maul, and Christina Maul, all of Waynoka, and Jacoby Maul of Oklahoma City; his brothers, Mike Maul and wife, Traci, John Maul, and Gary Maul, all of Dacoma; one granddaughter, Nicole Lynn Brown; nephew and nieces, Daniel Maul, Rylee Maul, and Jolynn Cellan; other relatives and friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
