Graveside services for Duyane Duncan will be 2:00 p.m. Thursday, September 29, 2011, at the Memorial Hill Cemetery with Rev. David Shaw officiating.  Arrangements are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Duyane Eugene Duncan, son of the late Archie and Daisy (Woolley) Duncan, was born August 11, 1933, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away September 25, 2011, at Rolla, Missouri, at the age of 78 years, 1 month, and 14 days.  
Duyane attended school in Waynoka and Colorado. He served in the United States Army. In 1985, he was united in marriage to Doris Keenan at Yukon, Oklahoma.  He was a heavy equipment operator for the city of Yukon until his retirement. A favorite hobby of his was electric trains.  
He was preceded in death by his parents and an aunt, Florence Like.  
Duyane is survived by his wife, Doris, of Rolla, Missouri; his son Eric Duncan of Rolla, Missouri; his daughter, Debi Baninger and husband, Todd, of Sacramento, California; two sisters Norma Cruisenberry of Halstead, Kansas, and Sharon Jones and husband, Lee, of Exeter, Missouri; two granddaughters, Danielle Alger of Sacramento, California, and Karlysa Harty; two aunts, Esther Clemence of Waynoka, and Retta Woolley of Waynoka; other relatives and friends.
