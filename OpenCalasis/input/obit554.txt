Funeral services for Marquitta "Ketta" Anne Howard will be 10:00 a.m. Thursday, December 11, 2008, at the Aline Methodist Church with Rev. Scott Heusel officiating.  Interment will be in the Lone Star Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Marquitta Anne Howard, daughter of the late Cecil Thompson, Jr. and Frances Mildred Thompson, was born April 10, 1951, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away December 7, 2008, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 57 years, 7 months, and 27 days.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Michael Howard.  
Marquitta graduated from Alva High School and later recieved her Associates Degree in Medical Office Management from Blinn College in Brenham, Texas.  On November 11, 2000, she was united in marriage to Michael Howard in Nevada.  She was employed for Alfalfa County Department of Human Services.  
She enjoyed all things electronic, especially computers, playing cards, and loved all animals, especially her dog, Juneau.  
Marquitta is survived by two children, Robert Allen and wife, Maggie, of Chicago, Illinois, and Tara Ruiz and husband, Cris, of Sealy, Texas; two sisters, Beverly Allen and husband, Nelson, of Enid, Oklahoma, and Tona Jebbia and husband, Steve of Dallas, Texas; and one brother, Tim Thompson and wife, Carla, of Richmond, Virginia; four grandchildren, Seth and Morgan Allen of Bryan, Texas, and Erik and Derik Ruiz of Sealy, Texas; several nieces and nephews; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the American Heart Association.
