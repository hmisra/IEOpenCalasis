Funeral services for Gene Howard Cundiff will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, October 23, 2007, at the Hardtner United Methodist Church with Pastor Elizabeth Kirkwood officiating.  Interment will be in the Hardtner Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Gene Howard Cundiff, son of the late Lynn and Eva Lorraine (Johnson) Cundiff, was born August 29, 1946, at Arkansas City, Kansas, and passed away October 19, 2007, at Pratt, Kansas, at the age of 61 years, 1 month, and 20 days.  
Gene attended school at Capron before graduating from Hardtner High School with the Class of 1964.  He attended Northwest Oklahoma State University.  On July 3, 1965, he was united in marriage to Barbara J. Eshleman in Enid, Oklahoma.  They made their home in Hardtner where he farmed and ranched.  
He was a member of the Hardtner United Methodist Church.  He enjoyed all types of hunting.  As an avid Kansas University basketball fan and Green Bay Packer football fan, he watched their games as often as possible.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by an infant son.  
Gene is survived by his wife, Barbara, of Hardtner; one son, Sean G. Cundiff of Wichita, Kansas; one daughter, Tammy Mustard and her husband, Rod, of Alva; one brother, Bill Cundiff of Hardtner, one sister, Mary Lynn Franco of Hardtner; two grandchildren, Kayla and Brandon Mustard of Alva; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home as a love fund for the family.
