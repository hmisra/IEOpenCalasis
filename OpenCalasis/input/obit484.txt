Funeral services for Barbara Lee Cox of Carmen, Oklahoma, will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, December 4, 2009, at the Wentworth Chapel in Carmen with Rev. John Bizzell officiating.  Interment will be in the Carmen City Cemetery under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC. 	 Barbara Lee Cox, daughter of the late William Virgil and Lois Maxine (Gagabin) Clark was born November 17, 1946, at Cherokee, Oklahoma, and passed away November 30, 2009, at St. John Medical Center in Tulsa, Oklahoma, at the age of 63 years and 13 days. 	 Barbara attended school in Pryor, Oklahoma. On May 29, 1964, she was united in marriage to Don Cox at Pryor. To this union three children were born: Donnie, Ginger, and Karla. They made their home in Pryor for many years before moving to Carmen five years ago. 	  
She was a member of the First United Methodist Church in Carmen.  Barbara enjoyed crafting, cooking, and canning. She also loved taking care of her goats. 	 She was preceded in death by her parents. 	 Barbara is survived by her husband, Don, of the home; one son, Donnie Cox and his wife, Cindy, of Pryor, Oklahoma; and two daughters, Ginger Hooker and her husband, Michael, of Bartlesville, Oklahoma, and Karla Livingston of Carmen, Oklahoma; two brothers, Bill Clark and his wife, Dorothy, of Van Buren, Missouri, and Boyd Clark and his wife, Louise, of Owasso, Oklahoma. 	 Also surviving are seven grandchildren, Nikki Cox, Montana Cox, Dakota Cox all of Pryor, Dayla Vaughn, Mackenzie Hooker, and Michelle Barnes and her husband, Nathan, all of Bartlesville, and Cindy Thomas and her husband, Chris, of Grove; and five great grandchildren, Alexis Cox, Brian Gilbert, Piper Barnes, Gracie and Isaiah Thomas. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Carmen First Responders or Friends of the Library.
