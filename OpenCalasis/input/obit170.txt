Funeral services for Elizabeth Ann "Beth? Zoth will be 3:00 p.m. Monday, May 17, 2010, at the Pleasant View Cemetery near Dacoma with Paul Cole, pastor of the Town and Country Christian Church, officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Elizabeth Ann Zoth, daughter of the late Logan Blaine and Elizabeth Ann (Carey) Wheat, was born March 23, 1920, in Woods County, Oklahoma, and passed away May 14, 2010, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 90 years, 1 month, and 21 days.  
Beth attended a rural school near Capron.  On March 16, 1942, she was united in marriage to Harry Smith.  They owned and operated a caf? in Ponca City for a time, then moved to Alva where they owned and operated Harry?s Diner in Alva.  Their diner was well known for the great hamburgers, chili and pies.  They also raised chickens in Arkansas for a time.  He preceded her in death on March 16, 1970, at which time she moved back to Alva.  
She was a member of the Town and Country Christian Church.  She loved reading her Bible,  yardwork and growing flowers.  As a writer, she wrote many poems and religious verses.  
On August 15, 1983, she was united in marriage to Raymond Ralph Zoth.  They lived in Alva until his death on February 28, 1996.  
Besides her parents and her husbands, she was preceded in death by three brothers, Samuel Wheat, Walter Wheat, and Blaine Wheat; and four sisters, Edith Schornick, Nellie Wheat, Dorothy Crouch, and Grace Wheat.  
Beth is survived by one brother, Paul Wheat and his wife, Betty, of Woodward, Oklahoma, four stepsons, Milton Zoth, Larry Zoth, Roger Zoth, and Richard Zoth; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Share Hospice or Town and Country Christian Church.
