Mass of Christian Burial for Bernard “Bernie” Beum will be 1:00 p.m. Monday, October 13, 2014, at Saint Peters Catholic Church in Woodward with Rev. Joe Arledge serving as the celebrant.  Interment will be in the Elmwood Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Bernard “Bernie” Beum, son of the late Oren Edwin and Catherine (Stottmann) Beum, was born March 5, 1956, in Woodward, and passed away October 10, 2014, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 58 years, 7 months, and 5 days.    
Bernie worked several jobs including oil field, heavy construction and most recently was working at Allied Custom Gypsum at Bouse Junction before retiring due to his health.  He was always happiest when he was running a tractor or bulldozer and driving semis.    
Bernie loved being with family and watching TV and had a love for watching every INDY car race.  He also enjoyed hunting and reloading his shells.  He also enjoyed riding motorcycles.    
He was preceded in death by his mother, Catherine Glover, his step father, James Glover; maternal grandparents, T.B. and Lucy (Noel) Stottmann; paternal grandparents, Paul and Mae (Moser) Beum; uncles, Ted Stottmann and Richard Highfill; his aunt, Lucy Pitcherello; cousin, Rick Highfill; and niece, Christie Ann Barnard.    
Those left to cherish his memory are two sisters, Cathy and husband, Steve Sarvash, of Mustang, Florence and husband, Raymond Barnard, of Mooreland; one brother, Greg Beum and wife, Nila, of Peyton, Colorado; one aunt, Rachel Highfill of Enid; two uncles, George Stottmann of Ringwood and John Stottmann of Rosston; as well as two nieces; four nephews, a great nephew, and several cousins.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Share Convalescent Home activity fund.
