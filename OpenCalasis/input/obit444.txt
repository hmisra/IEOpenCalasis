Graveside services for Floyd Raymond McGolden, Jr. will be 1:00 p.m. Tuesday, January 5, 2009, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Rev. Ken Higgins, pastor of the Waynoka Church of God officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Floyd Raymond McGolden, son of the late Floyd Raymond and Ethel Lucille (Bartlett) McGolden, was born July 25, 1947, in Enid, Oklahoma, and passed away December 31, 2009, at Haysville, Kansas, at the age of 62 years, 5 months, and 4 days.  
Floyd graduated from high school at Hayesville, Kansas with the Class of 1965.   He lived with his family in Enid and Wichita before moving to Haysville, where he lived most of his life.  He worked as a machinist at Chance Manufacturing.  He enjoyed going to Las Vegas.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by one sister, Mary Ethel McGolden.  
Floyd is survived by several cousins.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Hospice Care of Kansas for the Children?s Bereavement Camp.
