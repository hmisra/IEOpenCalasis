Funeral services for Tracy Lynn Criswell will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, October 8, 2008, at the Waynoka First Baptist Church with Rev. David Shaw officiating. Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka.  
Tracy Lynn Criswell, daughter of Elton and Mary Lou (Ramirez) Bybee, was born October 8, 1964, in Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away October 3, 2008, in Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 43 years, 11 months, 25 days.  
Tracy grew up in Waynoka and graduated from Waynoka High School. She went four years to Northwestern Oklahoma State University majoring in Pre-med before attending West Texas Women?s University where she received her training to be a Certified Pediatric Nurse Practitioner. She worked in Amarillo as a nurse practitioner for several years before returning to Waynoka last May.  
She was a member of Our Mother of Mercy Church and the Red Hat Society. She enjoyed photography, entertaining her nieces, nephews, and friends, and hunting. She also enjoyed watching Caleb race his stock car.  
She was preceded in death by her father-in-law, Kermit Criswell.  
Tracy is survived by her husband, Bruce, of Alva, one son, Caleb Criswell and fianc?, Cayenne Towns, of Alva; her parents, Elton and Mary Lou Bybee of Amarillo, Texas; one brother, Sloane Burling and wife, Melissa, of Mooreland, Oklahoma; two sisters, Toni Nethercutt of Waynoka, and Tina Gelnar of Waynoka; eight nieces and nephews, Kristin Ramos of Waynoka, Tyson Burling of Waynoka, Dillon Nethercutt of Waynoka, Welsey Gelnar of Mangum, Jacob Gelnar of Mangum, and Hunter Ross of Waynoka; three great nieces and nephews, Precious Ramos of Waynoka, Kaden Ramos of Waynoka, Riot Burling of Waynoka, Faith Burling of Mooreland, and Hannah Burling of Mooreland; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
