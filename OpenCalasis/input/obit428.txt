Funeral services for Ross Kenneth Lockhart will be 10:00 a.m. Thursday, April 21, 2011, at the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Ross Kenneth Lockhart, son of the late Kenneth Malone and Pearl Elizabeth (Golden) Lockhart, was born November 17, 1947, in Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away April 16, 2011, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 63 years, 4 months, and 29 days.  
Ross worked as a chemist at the Fort Supply Hospital.  
Ross is survived by one brother, Bruce Lockhart and wife, Linda, of Michigan and their children, Collin Lockhart of Michigan, and Leah Lockhart of Redwood, California; one stepson, Joseph Brosh, Jr.; three stepdaughters; numerous grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Remembrances may be shared with the family at www.marshallfuneralhomes.com.
