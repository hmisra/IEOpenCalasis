Graveside services for Margaret Evelyn Spencer will be 10:30 a.m. Friday, September 12, 2008, at the Alva Municipal Cemetery with Rev. Jason Dirks, pastor of the Lincoln Avenue Baptist Church in Woodward officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Margaret Evelyn Spencer, daughter of the late Benjamin and Lena Ellen (Rodenbach) Becker, was born March 19, 1921, on the farm near Reece, Kansas, and passed away September 9, 2008, at Woodward, Oklahoma, at the age of 87 years, 5 months, and 20 days.  
Margaret attended Reece school.  On March 20, 1943, she was united in marriage to Elza Spencer at the Green Valley Parsonage in Woods County, Oklahoma.  They lived in the Aline area where they farmed and ranched.  After Elza&amp;s death in 1991, she continued to farm and ranch until she moved to Woodward in 2006.  
She attended the Hopeton Wesleyan Church.  She loved gardening, caring for animals, and really enjoyed training horses.  
Besides her parents and her husband, she was preceded in death by one son, Elza Dean Spencer, one brother, Robert Becker; two sisters, Tommie McMoran and Pril Zahorsky; and one grandson, Justin Spencer.  
Margaret is survived by her daughter, Judy Ann Spencer of Woodward; son, Clyde Leon Spencer and wife, Helen, of Enid; two brothers, Ralph Becker and wife, Wilma, of Eureka, Kansas, and Jim Becker of Moline, Kansas; and two sisters, Christine Webb of Salem, Missouri, and Elizabeth Lohmann of Alva.  
Also surviving are six grandchildren, Troy Spencer and wife, Robin, of Enid, Mark Spencer of Enid; LaTonna Sims and husband, M.C., of Yantis, Texas, Reva Spencer of Irving, Texas, Gail Dean Spencer of Mooreland, Oklahoma, and Dean Spencer, Jr. and wife, Stephanie, of Purcell, Oklahoma; 10 great grandchildren; 6 great great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Lincoln Avenue Baptist Church.
