Funeral services for Eugene Terrel Means will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, May 28, 2010, at the Wentworth Chapel in Carmen with Rev. Dave Keller officiating.  Interment will be at the Pleasant Ridge Cemetery, Carmen, Oklahoma, under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC. 	 Eugene Terrel Means son of the late Alexander and Eva Jane (Ging) Means, was born on September 21, 1922, northeast of Carmen, Oklahoma, and passed away May 25, 2010, at Enid, at the age of 87 years, 8 months, and 4 days. 	 Eugene was raised in the Carmen area and attended Prairie Valley Grade School.  He graduated from Lambert High School in 1941. As a child he attended the Prairie Valley Methodist Church. On May 27, 1941, he was united in marriage to Rachel Alleen Schallner at Enid, Oklahoma.  To this union one son was born: Terry Means. They made their home in the Carmen area where he was a lifelong farmer. 	 Eugene was on the Carmen School Board for 15 years and was a board member of the Carmen Aline Coop for 9 years.  He enjoyed going to basketball games and watching his grandkids. 	 Besides his parents, Eugene was preceded in death by four brothers, Avery Means, Robert Means, Vernon Means, and Clifford Means; and one sister, Betty Jane Newman. 	 He is survived by his wife, Rachel, of the home; his son Terry Means and wife, Betty, of Carmen; two grandchildren, Kevin Means of Carmen and Kayli Means of Enid; numerous nephews and nieces, other relatives and friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for heart or diabetes research.
