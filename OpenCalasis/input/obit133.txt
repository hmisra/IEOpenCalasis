Graveside services for Zelma B. Ikler will be 1:00 p.m. Tuesday, February 23, 2010, at the Carmen City Cemetery with Rev. Kirk Board, pastor of the Carmen Christian Church officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC. 	 Zelma B. Ikler, daughter of the late Elisha and Myrtle Ellen (Gray) Morgan, was born October 18, 1925, at Helena, Oklahoma, and passed away February 18, 2010, at Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, at the age of 84 years and 4 months. 	 Zelma attended Carmen School.  On August 24, 1946, she was united in marriage to Gerald Ray (Dean) Ikler at Wichita, Kansas.  They lived in Wichita where she worked at Boeing manufacturing airplanes. She later moved to Enid, and spent the last five years in Oklahoma City.  	 She was a member of the Carmen Christian Church.  She enjoyed fishing, embroidery, and crocheting. 	 Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband; 10 brothers; and one sister. Zelma is survived by her son, Dick Ikler of Oklahoma City; one sister, Ila Mae Terrel of Enid, Oklahoma; three grandchildren,, Doug Ikler, Bryan Ikler, and Lisa Sevart; four great grandchildren; other relatives and friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to St. Jude?s Children?s Hospital.
