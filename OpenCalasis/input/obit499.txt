Graveside services for Madeline Mae Nighswonger will be 10:00 a.m. Monday, August 11, 2014, at the Capron Cemetery with Reverend Jerald Bushman officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Madeline Mae Nighswonger, daughter of the late Martin and Faye Lucille (Artis) Jensen, Sr., was born January 12, 1935, on the farm in Grant County, Oklahoma, and passed away August 8, 2014, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 79 years, 6 months, and 26 days.    
Madeline graduated from Manchester High School.  She attended LPN School at Woodward.  She was united in marriage to Jim Nighswonger.  She worked as an LPN at Share Convalescent Center from 1989 to 2001.    
She was a member of the Enid Maine Church of the Nazarene.    
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by one brother, Marten Jensen, Jr.; and one sister, Maxine Graves.    
Madeline is survived by two sons, Rick Starks and wife, Melodie, of Alva, and Richard Starks and wife, Carolyn, of Wichita Falls, Texas; three grandchildren, Damien Starks and wife, Brenda, of Alva, Kent Starks and wife, Stephanie, of Wichita, and Richelle Pohlman of Wichita; other relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Share Convalescent Home Activity Fund.
