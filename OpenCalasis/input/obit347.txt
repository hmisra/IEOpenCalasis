Graveside services for Gary Sims will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, March 11, 2009, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Rev. Ken Higgins, pastor of the Waynoka Church of God, officiating.  Interment will be in the Waynoka Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Gary Eugene Sims, son of the late Arnold (Jock) and Leona Waunita (Rawson) Sims, was born December 2, 1943, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away March 8, 2009, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, at the age of 65 years, 3 months, and 6 days.  
Gary attended Waynoka School.  He was united in marriage to Judy Moore.  He enlisted in the United States Army on March 23, 1965 and was honorably discharged on February 23, 1967.  On December 9, 1972, he was united in marriage to Shirley Hill.  Throughout his life, he worked several jobs, including as a mechanic, cowboy, roughneck, and on the pipeline, but most of his work was working for Woods County as a heavy equipment operator.  
He attended the Waynoka Church of God.  He enjoyed hunting, fishing, dancing, riding horses, being with friends, and watching western movies and reading western books.  
Gary is survived by one son, Gene Sims, of Yuma, Arizona; four daughters, Katrina Mukherjee and husband, Babul, of San Antonio, Texas, Teresa Howell and husband, Ronnie, of Waynoka, Regan Dawson and husband, Robert, of Woodward, and Jacky Sims of Waynoka; four brothers, Max Sims of Carmen, Ron Seaman of Austin, Texas, Keith Sims and wife, Sylvia, of Amarillo, and Jack Sims and wife, Patty, of Alva; 13 grandchildren, Heather, Nikhil, Cameron, and Nath Mukherjee, Leona, Dalores, Kyle, and Peyton Howell, Briana and Chase Dawson, and Dylan, Kaylee, and Lydon Sims; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka Nursing Center Activity Fund.
