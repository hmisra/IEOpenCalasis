Funeral services for Margaret Ruth "Midge? Reed will be 11:00 a.m. Monday, December 27, 2010, at the Alva First Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) with Rev. Ron Pingelton officiating.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  The casket will remain closed at the funeral home.  
Margaret Ruth "Midge? Reed, daughter of the late Harry Underwood and Louise (Wintrode) Underwood, was born May 22, 1925, at Wabash, Indiana, and passed away December 20, 2010, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 85 years, 6 months, and 28 days.  
Midge graduated from Alva High School with the class of 1942.  On July 11, 1952, she was united in marriage to Hugh J. "Sadie? Reed in Durant, Oklahoma.  She worked at the Alva General Hospital, was secretary for the Alva Chamber of Commerce and retired from Central National Bank.  
She was a member of the Alva First Christian Church (Disciples of Christ)  where she served as elder, Sunday school teacher, youth coordinator, and two terms as president of Church Women United.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Hugh J. "Sadie?, in 2002; her brother, Jack Underwood; and her sister, Betty Murphy.  
Midge is survived by two sons, Mike Miller, of Shawnee, H. J. Reed of Edmond; two daughters, Cheryl Adrian of Alva, and Jody McFarland of Enid;  her sister, Shirley McCormick of Yukon; eight grandchildren; Chad Miller of Norman, Andy Miller of Norman, Kim Birdsall of Point Pleasant, New Jersey, Chelsea Reed of Edmond, Melissa Reed of Edmond, Julie Smith of Marina Del Rey, California, Todd Adrian of Fitchburgh, Wisconsin, and Ashley Hellman of Waukomis, ten great grandchildren, Curren Smith, Tyson Adrian, Payton Adrian, Loren Miller, Elena Miller, Olivia Hyra, Taylor Miller, Sydney Miller, Reed Hellman, and Mikenley Hellman; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Lincoln Elementary School Library.
