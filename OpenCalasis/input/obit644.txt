Graveside services for Mildred “Millie” Marie Davidson will be 2:00 p.m.   Tuesday, December 11, 2012, at the Square Cedar Cemetery with Rev. John Bizzell officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC.    
Mildred “Millie” Marie Davidson, daughter of the late Roy Lee and Alice Elizabeth (Moring) Walker, was born October 14, 1937, in Anthony, Kansas, and passed away December 7, 2012, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 75 years, 1 month, and 23 days.    
On April 27, 1987, she was united in marriage to Wayne Davidson in Anthony, Kansas.  They made their home in Carmen.  Millie was blessed with four step children and one adopted daughter.  She often helped Wayne pour concrete.    
Millie attended the Carmen Methodist Church.  She enjoyed playing cards and dominoes and doing any kind of crafts.  She also enjoyed cooking Sunday dinners and get togethers.    
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by four brothers, Leroy, Jack, Edward, and Robert.    
She leaves her husband, Wayne, of Carmen, four daughters, Twila and husband, Billy Case, of Carmen, Tina and husband, Jimmy Rion of Tennessee, Tammy and husband, Shane Garner, of Kansas, and Stephanie and husband, George Liebsch, of Carmen; one son, Todd Harrelson and wife, Doddy, of Idaho; two sisters, Betty Hawley of Carmen and Rosie Besore and husband, Bob, of Texas; two brothers, Johnnie Walker and wife, Mary Ann, of Colorado, and Jim Walker and wife, Pat, of Texas; nine grandchildren, Tarryn Davidson, Jaden Liebsch, Paityn Liebsch, Angelica Rion, Cody  and Tyler Garner, J. R. Davidson, Della Case, and Stella Harrelson; other relatives and many friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
