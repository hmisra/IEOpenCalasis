Funeral services for Nadine Wiechman will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, January 8, 2010, at the Zion Lutheran Church with Pastor Mark Bersche officiating.  Interment will be in the Lutheran Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Nadine Marie Wiechman, daughter of the late Cecil and  Jewell (Boydstun) Garrett was born on September 1, 1929, at Lexington, Oklahoma and died January 5, 2010, at Kiowa, Kansas, at the age of 80 years, 4 months, and 4 days.    
She graduated from high school in Lexington, Oklahoma. She became a Certified Nurse?s Aid and worked in various hospitals and nursing homes  around the area.  Nadine married Robert Lee Wiechman on October 2, 1960, at Derby, Kansas.  They made their home in northwestern Oklahoma and southern Kansas moving to Alva in 1994.  They have lived the past two years in Kiowa.  
She was a member of the Zion Lutheran Church in Alva and enjoyed reading, crocheting, cooking, talking and story telling.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by a brother Edwin Cecil Garrett.  
Nadine is survived by her husband, Robert of Kiowa, Kansas; three sons, Myron Wiechman and his wife, Jerri, of Manchester, Oklahoma, Barry Wiechman and his wife, Vanita, of Winfield, Kansas, and Thomas Wiechman and his wife, Lisa, of Rogers, Arkansas; one daughter, Roberta Fischer of Kiowa, Kansas; two brothers, Vernon Garrett of Lexington, Oklahoma, and Kenneth Garrett of Sulphur, Oklahoma; and one sister, Gayle Garrett of Norman, Oklahoma.  
Also surviving are six grandchildren, Josh Wiechman, of Wichita, Kansas, Jessica Wiechman, Cherokee, Oklahoma, Wesley Wiechman of Rogers, Arkansas, Hannah Wiechman of Rogers, Arkansas, Brayden Wiechman of Rogers, Arkansas and Richard Fischer of Kiowa, Kansas; two great grandchildren, other relatives and friends.  
Memorials may be made through the funeral home to the Zion Lutheran Church.
