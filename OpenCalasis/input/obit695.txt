Funeral services for James Henry Ball will be 2:00 p.m. Thursday, February 4, 2010, at the Waynoka First Baptist Church with Rev. David Shaw officiating.  Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
James Henry Ball, son of the Ernestine Pixler and the late Andrew Jackson Ball, was born December 29, 1958, at Grants, New Mexico, and passed away February 1, 2010, at his home in Waynoka, Oklahoma, at the age of 51 years, one month and two days.  
James attended school in Progress, Mississippi, and El Nido, California before graduating from Dos Palso High School.  He lived for several years in Oklahoma City where he managed a horse ranch.  Twelve years ago, he moved to Waynoka where he worked at Little Sahara State Park, Jiffy Trip and Ampride.  He was working at Little Sahara Power Sport at the time of his death.  
James was raised in the Pentecostal Church of God.  He loved working with animals, and collecting miniature cars.  
He was preceded in death by his father.  
James is survived by his mother, Ernestine Pixler and her husband, Alvin, of Waynoka; two brothers, Andrew Ball and wife, Debbie, of Lowell, Arkansas, and Donald Ball and wife, Carolene, of Warr Acres, Oklahoma; two sisters, Andrea Young and husband, David, of Rogers, Arkansas, and Carolyn Lockwood and husband, David, of Albany, Oregon; three step-brothers, Stanley Pixler and wife, Eva, of Ceour  d?Alene, Idaho, Dan Pixler and wife, Pamela, of Mariposa, California, Alan Pixler and wife, Louise, of Cincinnati, Ohio; and one step-sister, Geraldine Johnson and husband, John, of Post Falls, Idaho; his soul brother, Michael Hooper; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Waynoka E. M. T.?s or Creature Concerns.
