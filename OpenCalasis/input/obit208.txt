Memorial service for Janette Diane (Jan) Little will be 11:00 a.m. Saturday, August 9, 2008, at the Alva First Baptist Church with Dr. Greg Hook officiating.  She was cremated at her request under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Janette Diane Little, daughter of the late Dwight Clifford and Estella Mae (Hada) Little, was born November 2, 1948, at Hardtner, Kansas, and passed away July 14, 2008, at Share Convalescent Center, at the age of 59 years, 8 months, and 12 days.  
Jan attended Greenleaf Grade School and graduated from Alva High School.  After graduating from high school, she attended college at Miltonvale Wesleyan College in Kansas and graduated with her Masters Degree from the University of Northern Iowa in Cedar Rapids.  She taught hearing impaired children in Waterloo, Iowa, and Salina, Kansas, and her students were very important to her.  She lived in Enid at the Sterling House for three years before moving to Alva in 2001.  
She attended the Alva First Baptist Church and was a member of the National Multiple Sclerosis Society.  Jan enjoyed traveling, reading, playing the piano and horseback riding.  She liked being outdoors and was very athletic before being diagnosed with multiple sclerosis at age 16.  
Besides her parents Jan was preceded in death by one brother, Stanley.  
She is survived by two brothers, Ron Little and his wife, Sharol, of Estes Park, Colorado and Warren Little and his wife, Judy, of Alva, Oklahoma; two sisters, Carolyn Streck and her husband, Larry, of Troup, Texas and Sherry Hobbie and her husband, Gordon, of Altamonte Springs, Florida; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to World Hope International or the Oklahoma Multiple Sclerosis Chapter.
