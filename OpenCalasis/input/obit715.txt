Memorial services for Elizabeth Miller Rees will be at 12:00 p.m., Friday, May 30, 2008, at the St. Patrick&amp;s Catholic Church in Dallas, Texas.  Celebrant will be the Rev. Josef Vollmer-King.  Arrangements were made by Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Elizabeth Miller Rees, daughter of the late Harold and Elizabeth Miller, was born in New York City on October 26, 1919.  Elizabeth (Bette) passed away at Share Convalescent Home in Alva, Oklahoma, on Saturday, May 24, 2008, at the age of 88 years.  
Bette was united in marriage to Frank W. Rees on February 14, 1942, in New York City.  Elizabeth and Frank lived in Now York where sons Frank, Jr. And Michael were born.  In 1948, the family moved to Texas where their daughter, Mary Ellen, was born.  
During her 65?Â« years of marriage to Frank, they lived in New York, Texas, Oklahoma, California, and Arizona.  Bette was a homemaker and school teacher.  She retired from teaching school in 1979.  During her retirement in Tucson, Bette attended art classes and volunteered at one of the local high schools.  In 1996, she was honored as the Volunteer of the Year for Mineral Wells High School.  
She was preceded in death by her husband on September 19, 2007, and one daughter, Elizabeth G. Rees in 1945.
