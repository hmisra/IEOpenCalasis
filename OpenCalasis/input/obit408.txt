Graveside services for James Richard "Jim? Zook will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, October 20, 2006, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka.  
James Richard "Jim? Zook, son of the late Jacob Irvin and Hannah (Kagarice) Zook, was born October 18, 1912, and passed away October 18, 2006, on his 94th birthday at Woodward, Oklahoma.  
Jim lived his entire life in the Waynoka area.  On April 23, 1934, he was united in marriage to Verda Pauline (Grant) Zook at Alva, Oklahoma.  Jim was a retired employee of Woods County District 3, a farmer and oil field worker.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by his wife, Verda; six brothers, Albert (Bud) Zook, Chester Zook, Fred Zook, Harry Zook, Howard Zook, Ralph Zook; and three sisters, Bess Robinson, Lillian Zook, and Madeana Spears.  
Jim is survived by two sons, Richard Zook and his wife, Ava, of Waynoka, Gary Zook of Waynoka; one daughter, Rosa Lee Whipple and her husband, Dennis of Waynoka; 8 grandchildren, 15 great grandchildren; other family members and friends.  
Remembrances may be shared with the family at www.marshallfuneralhomes.com.
