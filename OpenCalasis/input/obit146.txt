Graveside services for Edna Pauline Stark will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, February 27, 2007, at Memorial Hill Cemetery with Ray Hull officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Edna Pauline Stark, daughter of the late Garrett Ben and Jane (Reinard) Boland, was born October 22, 1912, in the Big Timber area in Woods County, Oklahoma, and passed away February 24, 2007, at Share Convalescent Center in Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 94 years, 4 months, and 2 days.  
Edna attended rural school southeast of Waynoka.  She was united in marriage to John Henry Stark in California.  They lived north of Waynoka where they farmed and ranched.  She worked hard tending for their cows, hogs, and chickens.  They later moved into Waynoka.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, John; four brothers, Earl, Sam, Jake, and Jess Boland; and one sister, Gertie Bixler.  
Edna is survived by several nieces and nephews; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka EMTs.
