Graveside services for Virginia Winston “Virgie” Snyder will be 10:00 a.m. Saturday, September 7, 2013, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Rev. Lester Lowry officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  	  Virginia Winston “Virgie” Snyder, daughter of the late Frank Leo and Lottie Mae (Parker) Green, was born October 10, 1921, in Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away September 3, 2013, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 91 years, 10 months, and 23 days.  	  She was raised by her grandparents, William and Lucinda Parker, and graduated from Waynoka High School.  On August 3, 1945, she was united in marriage to Raymond A. Snyder.  	  Virgie was a member of the Waynoka Assembly of God Church where she taught Sunday School and had many interests in the youth groups of the church.  Her love of music and the ability to play all kinds of instruments led her to teach music to many children in Waynoka.  	  Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Raymond; two sisters, Velma Mae Green and Tracie Mae Green; one brother, Kenneth Clayton Green; her niece, Sherlene Coulter; and her nephew, Sheldon Snyder.  	  Virgie is survived by her nephew, Erl F. Snyder, Sr. of Duncan; several great nieces and nephews, including Sharesa Gregg of Enid, and Erl Snyder, Jr. of Oklahoma City.  	  Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with expenses..
