Funeral services for Raymond Clyde Phillips will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, January 8, 2008, at the Waynoka Church of God with Pastor John Brint officiating.  Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Raymond Clyde Phillips, son of the late Frank and Edith (Duncan) Phillips, was born 12/31/1924, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away January 5, 2008, at Integris Bass Baptist Medical Center in Enid, at the age of 83 years and 4 days.  
Raymond attended Waynoka School.  At the age of 17, he enlisted in the United States Navy where he served 2 years and 9 months until his medical discharge.  On June 28, 1952, he was united in marriage to LaEtta Rose Morris at Medicine Lodge Kansas.  He worked on the railroad,14 years for National Gypsum Company in Medicine Lodge, and as a custodian at NWOSU.  He returned to Waynoka in 1974 and was a member of the Waynoka Church of God.  
He was preceded in death by two brothers, Marvin and Richard Phillips, and one sister, Myrtle Decker.  
Raymond is survived by his wife, LaEtta, of Waynoka; two sons, Delbert Phillips of St. Louis, Missouri, and Verl Phillips and his wife, Sheila, of Waynoka; one daughter, Victoria Rose Borio and her husband, Ernie, of Owasso, Oklahoma; three grandchildren, Marlo Swanson and her husband, Tom, of Owasso, Derrick Phillips of Waynoka, and Starla Phillips of Stillwater; six great grandchildren; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka Church of God.
