Memorial services for Delbert Leon Phillips will be 2:00 p.m. Monday, January 25, 2010, at the Waynoka Church of God with Rev. Ken Higgins, pastor of the Waynoka Church of God officiating, under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Delbert Leon Phillips, son of LaEtta Rose (Morris) Phillips and the late Raymond Clyde Phillips, was born February 28, 1953, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away January 20, 2010, at Florissant, Missouri, at the age of 56 years, 10 months, and 23 days.  
After Delbert graduated from Alva High School, he served in the United States Air Force.  He lived in Tulsa for about 20 years where he worked for McDonnell-Douglas and Boeing.  He lived in Louisiana for about 2 years where he met his future wife, Debbie Reeves.  They moved to St. Louis where he again worked for Boeing.  After being laid off at Boeing he started working on medical equipment.   
He especially enjoyed fishing, but also enjoyed hunting.  During the last few years he had taken up golf.    
Besides his father, he was preceded in death by his wife, Debbie, on April 10, 2006.  
Delbert is survived by his mother, LaEtta, of Waynoka; one brother, Verl Phillips and fianc?, Audra, of Waynoka; and one sister Vicky Borio and husband, Ernie, of Owasso; two nieces, Marlo Swanson and Starla Phillips; one nephew, Derrick Phillips; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka Church of God where his family has been active.
