Graveside services for Ralph V. Wheat will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, February 20, 2008, at the Fairvalley Cemetery with Reverend John Clapp, pastor of the Bible Baptist Church officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Ralph V. Wheat, son of the late John and Clara (Eckels) Wheat, was born August 20, 1929, in Woods County, Oklahoma, and passed away February 15, 2008, at Share Convalescent Home in Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 78 years, 5 months, and 26 days.  
Ralph attended McKeever School.  He served over six years in the United States Army.  On May 19, 1951, he was united in marriage to Evelyn Ella Feitzinger at Lyndhurst, New Jersey.  He drove a truck until he retired in 1989.  He moved to Alva in October, 2002, with his daughter and her husband and granddaughter.  
Ralph was a member of Alva Masonic Lodge #105, a 32nd Degree Guthrie Scottish Rite Consistory, India Shrine, and American Legion Post #92.  He loved birds and dogs.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by his wife, Evelyn and two sister, Marlene and Lenora.  
Ralph is survived by his daughter, Judy Hawksford and her husband, Arthur, of Alva; two brothers, Walter Combs and his wife, Barbara, of Rimrock, Arizona and Joe Combs and his wife, Florence, of Springdale, Arkansas; four sisters, Lorna Susie Canaan and her husband, Donald, of Alva, Rosemary Patton of Mira Loma, California, Virginia Lever, of Pueblo West, Colorado, and Esther Mayfield of LaJolla, California; two grandchildren, Cherie Leach and her husband, Antony of Oklahoma City, and Arthur Carl Hawksford, Jr. of Laurence Harbor, New Jersey; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Masonic Lodge #105 (Alva)and #307 (Cherokee).
