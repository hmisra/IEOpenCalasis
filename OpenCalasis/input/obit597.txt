Funeral services for Vera Odele Wheat will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, March 31, 2009, at the Town and Country Christian Church with Rev. Paul Cole officiating.  Interment will be in the Riverview Cemetery at Kiowa under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Vera Odele Wheat, daughter of the late Oscar Asbery and Florence May (Wright) Newton, was born July 12, 1913, at Kiowa, Kansas, and passed away March 27, 2009, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 95 years, 8 months, and 15 days.  
Vera graduated from Kiowa High School with the Class of 1932.  On July 30, 1933, she was united in marriage to Otis V. Wheat at Medicine Lodge, Kansas.  They owned and operated a dairy farm where she milked lots of cows.  They retired in 1971.  
She was a member of the Town and Country Christian Church.  She was a strong Christian and was faithful to the Lord and her church.  She loved to read and also volunteered as a Pink Lady at Share Nursing Home.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by two brothers, Paul Asbery Newton and Robert Dean Newton; and one sister, Ellen Nadine Gabreel.  
Vera is survived by several nieces and nephews; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Beadles Nursing Home Activity Fund.
