Funeral services for Sandra Sue Marteney will be 10:00 a.m. Thursday, June 20, 2013, at the Alva First Assembly of God Church with Doctor Ken Stanton officiating.  There will be a visitation with the family Wednesday, June 19, 2013, from 12:00 p.m. to 9:00 p.m. at Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  Interment will be in the Cairo Cemetery near Pratt, Kansas, under the direction of Best Funeral Services and Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Sandra Sue Marteney, daughter of the late Lloyd and Nada June Cox, was born July 13, 1943, at Pratt, Kansas, and passed away June 15, 2013, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 69 years, 11 months, and 2 days.    
Sandy graduated from Sawyer High School in 1962.  On June 2, 1962 she was united in marriage to Roy Marteney in Pratt, Kansas.  She was an active member of the First Assembly of God Church in Alva.  Sandy enjoyed spending time with her grandchildren, quilting, and traveling.      
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Roy W. Marteney; and two brothers, John Clifford Cox and Donald Lee Cox, Sr.    
Sandra is survived by her daughter, Patti Kilgore of Alva; her son, Scott Marteney and wife, Stephanie, of Alva; her brother, Kerry Cox and wife, Janet, of Pratt, Kansas, her brother, Dean Cox and wife, Marilyn, of Preston, Kansas; her sister, Linda Kent and husband, Brad of Hutchinson, Kansas; her sister-in-law, Marilyn K. Cox, of Preston, Kansas; her grandchildren, Tiffani, Ericka, Ashlee, Mitchel, Dalton, and Dylen.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the American Cancer Society.
