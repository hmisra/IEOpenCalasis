Memorial services for Shereena Sue Montano will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, January 27, 2015, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Ray Hull officiating.  She was cremated under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.    
Shereena Sue Montano, daughter of Roscoe and the late Darlene Sue (McNally) Like, was born December 17, 1969, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away January 17, 2015, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 45 years and 1 month.     
Shereena graduated from Waynoka High School with the Class of 1988.  On December 2, 1998, she was united in marriage to Johnny Ray Montano at Buffalo, Oklahoma.  They made their home in Waynoka where she worked as a sales clerk in Alva.    
She attended the Church of Christ.  She loved to cook, sew, fish, and take care of her family.    
She was preceded in death by her mother, Darlene Like.    
Shereena is survived by her husband, Johnny Ray Montano of Waynoka; three children, Daxton Wayne Montano, Skyler Don Montano, and Crystal Brookes Montano, all of Waynoka; her father, Roscoe Like and wife, Enola, of Waynoka; one brother, Melvin Like and wife, Kelly, of Waynoka; other relatives and many friends.    
In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
