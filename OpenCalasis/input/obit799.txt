Funeral services for Edith Mae (Rader) Rieger will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, September 7, 2010, at the Church of God in Alva with Rev. Bob Brown officiating. Interment will be in Fairview Cemetery, north of Alva, under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home. 	 Edith Mae (Rader) Rieger, daughter of the late Aaron and Ethel (Bowers) Rader, was born May 10, 1919, in Carroll County, Arkansas, and went to her heavenly home on September 4, 2010. She passed away at Share Convalescent Home in Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 91 years, 3 months, and 25 days. 	 Edith graduated from Capron High School and attended one year at Northwestern Oklahoma State College.  On July 3, 1940, she was united in marriage to Edwin Rieger in Wellington, Kansas.  They made their home in the Alva area where Edith worked at various jobs including Cooks Shoe Store and Magnison?s Grocery Store.  One of her favorite jobs was cooking at the VIP Supper Club where she took pride in serving her customers great food.  After the closing of the VIP, she continued cooking for the Lutheran Daycare and later served at the Alva Public Schools. 	 She was a faithful member of the Alva Church of God.  Edith loved to bowl on Wednesday night with her friends, play cards, dance and listen to western music. 	 Besides her parents and her husband, Edwin, she was preceded in death by one daughter, Carol; a sister, Wyveta Calhoun; and two brothers, Raymond Rader and Wayne Rader. 	 Edith is survived by her daughter, Sherry Evans and husband, Melvin, of Alva; a niece, Barbara Duncan and husband, Adren, of Little Rock, Arkansas; and Romeo, her favorite granddog that lives with Sherry. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Church of God Building Fund or the Oklahoma Heart Association.
