Funeral services for Russell Glen Smith will be 2:00 p.m. Saturday, June 16, 2007, at the Hopeton Wesleyan Church with Rev. Marion Hadsel officiating.  Interment will be in the Preston Hill Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
There will be a visitation time at the funeral home with the family at 6:00 pm, Friday, June 15, 2007. 	 Russell Glen Smith, son of Glen and Marilyn (Brehmer) Smith, was born May 30, 1966, in Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away June 14, 2007, at Tulsa, Oklahoma, at the age of 41 years and 16 days. 	 Russell attended School in Sapulpa and Lawton before graduating from Carmen-Dacoma High School.  He then attended Oklahoma State University Tech at Okmulgee where he learned to be a mechanic.  On October 10, 2004, he was united in marriage to Donna Shive at Hopeton.  He lived in Dacoma most of his life where he served on the Volunteer Fire Department. 	 He was a member of the Hopeton Wesleyan Church where he was the drummer for the Praise and Worship Team.  He also enjoyed sailing and playing the drums. 	 Russell is survived by his wife, Donna Smith of Dacoma; one son, Cameron Smith of Lewis, Kansas; one daughter, Autumn Smith of Lewis, Kansas; one sister, Ann Becker of Shawnee, Oklahoma; his dad, Glen Smith, and his wife, Nancy, of Carmen, his mother, Marilyn Davison, and her husband, Jim, of Alva; his grandmother, Ella Brehmer, of Alva; one step-son, Daniel Winters of Dacoma; one step-daughter, Melissa Winters of Dacoma; one step-brother, Dale Frey and his wife, Melinda, of Oklahoma City; three step-sisters, Dian Noyes and her husband, Marvin of Carmen, Dana Ford and her husband, Derek, of Woodward, and Debbie Brown and her husband, Sequoyah, of Booker, Texas; other relatives and many friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the family for funeral expenses.
