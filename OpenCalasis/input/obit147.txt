Memorial services for Kurt Douglas Klein will be 2:00 p.m. Monday, July 21, 2014, at Generations Church in Waynoka with Charles Newton officiating.  Kurt was cremated at his request under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  Graveside memorial service will be 10:30 a.m., Wednesday, July 23, 2014, at the Oxford Cemetery, Oxford, Kansas.    
Kurt Douglas Klein, son of Lloyd Dean and Mildred Lodean (Loften) Klein, was born June 20, 1963, at Winfield, Kansas, and passed away July 13, 2014, at Oklahoma City, at the age of 51 years and 23 days.    
After Kurt graduated from Oxford High School and attended Cowley County Community College at Arkansas City, Kansas, he worked several years as a machinist and maintenance mechanic at Boeing in Wichita.  He moved to Waynoka where he was employed as the Water Superintendent at the City of Waynoka.  On February 24, 2014, he was united in marriage to Sharlotte Bolar at Taloga.    
Kurt was a member of the Oxford Methodist Church.  He was also a member the Oxford Masonic Lodge #165 and transferred to Waynoka Masonic Lodge #422,  32nd Degree Mason of the Scottish Rite Center in Wichita.    
He was preceded in death by his mother, Mildred Lodean Klein; and one brother, Victor Klein.    
Kurt is survived by his wife, Sharlotte, of Waynoka; his father, Lloyd Klein and his wife, Diana, of Udall, Kansas; one daughter, Myka Seiger and husband, Waylon, of Waynoka; one brother, Jerry Klein of Oxford, Kansas; two grandchildren; Shelby and Landon Seiger of Waynoka; other relatives and many friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka EMTs.
