Memorial services for Frances Ann Welch will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, April 14, 2010, at the Alva First Christian Church (Disciples of Christ) with Rev. Ron Pingelton officiating.  She was cremated at her request.  Arrangements are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Frances Ann Welch, daughter of the late John Welch and Bertha (Hankins) Cameron, was born June 9, 1921, northwest of Alva, and passed away April 12, 2010, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 88 years, 10 months, and 3 days.  
After Fran graduated from Alva High School she attended Northwestern Oklahoma State University.  On July 9, 1938, she was united in marriage to Doss Gourley.  She was a longtime employee with Woods County as Deputy Court Clerk and later with the OSU County Extension Service Center.  She was also employed for three years with the OSU Extension Center as 4-H secretary in Oklahoma City.  She later married Cecil Welch.   
Fran loved gardening, playing the piano, playing Yahtzee, knitting, shooting pool and throwing darts.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband Cecil Welch; her sister, Jessie Crouse and her brother, Robert Cameron.  
Fran is survived by two sons, Rod Gourley and wife, Dian, of Alva, and Doug Gourley and wife, Karen of Woodward; two granddaughters, Lendi Gourley, Alva and fianc?, Colby Clodfelter of Lawton, Oklahoma, and Jamie Peters and husband, Mark, of Turpin, Oklahoma; one great grandson, Gage Dosson Peters; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Alva First Christian Church or the Women of the Moose.
