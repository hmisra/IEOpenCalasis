Graveside services for Opal May Kay will be 2:00 p.m. Saturday, January 16, 2010, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Rev. David Shaw officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Opal May Kay, daughter of the late Robert Lee and Willie May (Campbell) Holmes, was born March 5, 1916, at Marlin, Texas, and passed away January 13, 2010, at Weleetka, Oklahoma, at the age of 93 years, 10 months, and 8 days.  
Opal graduated from high school at Kosse, Texas.  On April 27, 1945, she was united in marriage to William Lewis Kay.  They made their home in Waynoka where she was a member of the Cimarron Valley Club.  After his death, she moved to Oklahoma City, Eufaula, and then Weleetka.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Lewis, on August 11, 1972; and three sisters, Ruby Reddock, Jewel Williams, and Gladys Sanderson.  
Opal is survived by one son, Robert Kay and wife, Janet, of Porum, Oklahoma; three grandchildren; seven great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for Alzheimer?s Research.
