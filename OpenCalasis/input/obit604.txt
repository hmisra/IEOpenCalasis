Funeral services for Iona Cynthia "Onie" Delano will be 10:00 a.m. Monday, July 26, 2010, at the Carmen Christian Church with Rev. Kirk Board officiating. Interment will be in the Eagle Chief Cemetery under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC. 	 Iona Cynthia "Onie" Delano, daughter of the late Daniel Boone and Bengia Elizabeth (Garvin) Potts, was born August 20, 1916, southwest of Carmen, in Alfalfa County, Oklahoma, and passed away July 23, 2010, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 93 years, 11 months, and 3 days. 	 Onie graduated from Carmen High School with the Class of 1934. On May 29, 1938, she was united in marriage to Lyle Dammond Delano, at Aline, Oklahoma. They lived in Carmen where they  farmed, and owned and operated a caf?. She also worked at the Carmen Headlight and was a Medical Aide at the Carmen Home. 	 She was a member of the Carmen Christian Church. She enjoyed sewing and quilting. 	 Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Lyle; and an infant daughter. 	 Onie is survived by two sons, Dennis Delano and wife, Joyce, of Arcadia, and Bill Delano and wife, Linda, of Carmen; one step daughter, Glenda Hazelton, of Surprise, Arizona; one sister, Janeauita Ging of Waynoka; six grandchildren, Leslie Hazelton of Haltom City, Texas, Sheri Hazelton of Irving, TX; Amber Douglas and husband, Tim, of Broken Arrow, Trent Delano and wife, Janna, of Aline, Craig Delano and wife, Christy, of Oklahoma City, and Nichole Delano of Arcadia; four great grandchildren, Alexis and Avery Douglas, and Payton and Titus Delano; other relatives and friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Carmen First Responders.
