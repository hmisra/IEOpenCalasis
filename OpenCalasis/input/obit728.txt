Memorial services for Sadie Goodwin,103, will be 10:00 a.m. Saturday, May 25, 2013, at the Kingdom Hall of Jehovah’s Witnesses in Alva, Oklahoma with Clayton Forrest officiating.  Arrangements are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Sadie Goodwin, daughter of the Harry and Smithy Horne, was born September 9, 1909, in Fountain, North Carolina, and passed away May 11, 2013, at St. Mary’s Hospital in Enid, Oklahoma.    
She was the loving wife of the late Charles Goodwin of Greenville, South Carolina.  She worked at the Federal Telephone and Radio Corp. Of New Jersey for 20 years.    
She was preceded in death by three children, Don Wayne Goodwin, Alonzo Williams, and Donna Hawkins; and seventeen siblings.    
She is survived by three children, Gerald Purvis of Avenel, New Jersey, Joyce Griffin of Atlanta, Georgia, and Velma Forrest of Alva, Oklahoma; one sister, Nettie Elizabeth Barnes of Walstonburg, North Carolina.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Kingdom’s Hall of Jehovah’s Witnesses.
