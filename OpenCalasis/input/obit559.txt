Funeral services for Eldor Erich Stelling will be 10:00 a.m. Tuesday, July 20, 2010, at the Zion Lutheran Church with Pastor Mark Bersche officiating.  Interment will be in the Zion Lutheran Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Eldor Erich Stelling, son of John and Lina (Schroeder) Stelling, was born November 5, 1917, at Frohna, Missouri, and passed away July 17, 2010, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 92 years, 8 months, and 12 days.  
Eldor attended school at Point Rest, Missouri.  In 1945, he moved to Alva and started working for Alva Farmer?s Co-op shortly thereafter.  On March 9, 1952, he was united in marriage to Esther Lohmann at Alva, Oklahoma.  In 1953 he began his farming operation and started painting for various homeowners.  
He was a member of the Zion Lutheran Church in Alva where he served the church in several capacities.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by one daughter, Sharon; and four brothers, Edmund, Alonzo, Clarence, and Harold.  
Eldor is survived by his wife, Esther, of Alva; one son, Randy, and wife, Kim, of Alva; two daughters, Glenda King and husband, Rob, of Ozark, Missouri, and Sandra Brace and husband, Mark, of Lawton, Oklahoma; seven grandchildren, Amanda Nichols and husband, Jon, of Ozark, Missouri, Matthew King of Ozark, Missouri, Cody Stelling and wife, Merilee, of Woodward, Trey Stelling of Alva, Alissa Stelling of Alva, Michael Brace of Oklahoma City, and Kaylee Brace of Lawton; three great grandchildren, Haleigh Nichols, and Addison and Cooper Stelling; other relatives and many friends.  
In lieu of flowers, the family requests memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Zion Lutheran Church.
