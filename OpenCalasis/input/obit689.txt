Graveside services for Devann Maunee Cottingham will be 2:00 p.m. Saturday, January 26, 2013, at the Mooreland Cemetery with Pastor David Houchin officiating. Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.    
Devann Maunee Cottingham, daughter of Michelle Rene (Hand) Cottingham and James Cottingham, was born July 5, 1990, Denver, Colorado, and passed away January 24, 2013, at her home in Mooreland, Oklahoma, at the age of 22 years, 6 months, and 19 days.    
Devann graduated from Mooreland High School. She enjoyed laughter, music, lights, balloons, dancing, school and hanging with her best friend Sydney.  In the short time Devann was in our lives she touched our hearts and souls and her presence in our life is a gift we will never forget.    
Devann is survived by her mother, Michelle Cottingham, of Mooreland; her father, James Cottingham, of Santa Maria, California and Tim Farrar of Tupelo, Mississippi; sister, Brookee Lynn Cottingham-Roper, two brothers, Garret Cottingham and Gage Cottingham; her grandparents James and Gerry Hand; her Uncle James Bud Hand, her nephew Stetson Roper, her niece Jalynn Roper and her best friend, Sydney Rienhart; other many relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home or Central National Bank of Enid in Mooreland for funeral expenses.
