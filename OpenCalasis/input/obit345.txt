Memorial services for Viola Linda "Susie? Trekell will be 1:00 p.m. Monday, December 28, 2009, at the Bible Baptist Church with Rev. John Clapp officiating.  Inurnment will follow at the Pleasant View Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  She was cremated at her request.  
Viola Linda "Susie? Trekell, daughter of the late Harvey Melton and Jessie Olive (Hammond) Trekell, was born January 28, 1937, at the Alva Regional Hospital in Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away December 22, 2009, at Share Convalescent Center in Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 72 years, 10 months, and 24 days.  
Susie attended school only through the 7th grade due to the school not having disability access.  She lived all of her life in Alva and loved all of the children that came in and out of her life, especially her grandchildren.  She enjoyed crocheting and loved plants and gardens.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by two brothers, Russell "Bud? Trekell and Marshall Wayne Trekell; and five sisters, Fern Alice Mitchell, Florence Pearl Clinesmith, Inez Irene Osborne, Esther Ann Icke, and Phyllis Jeanne Terrel.  
Susie is survived by one daughter, Marie Ent and husband, Keith, of Alva; three brothers, Robert William Trekell and wife, Elaine, of Alva, Allen Trekell and wife, Paula, of Casa Grande, Arizona, and Harvey Melton "John? Trekell, Jr. and wife, Glenda, of Kaw City, Oklahoma; four grandchildren, Sharra Lanea Fulkerson of Alva, Brandon Michael Fulkerson of Alva, Robert Isaac Ent of Skiatook, and Jacob Keith Ent of Skiatook; many nieces and nephews; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Share Hospice.
