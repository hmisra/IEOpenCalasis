Graveside services for Ivan Metcalf will be 2:30 p.m. Sunday, March 15, 2009, at the Timberlake Cemetery near Helena with Rev. William S. Kalfas, pastor of the Alva Church of the Nazarene, officiating.  Interment will be in the Timberlake Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva. 	 Ivan Ray Metcalf, son of the late Emery L. and Jessie (Lemon) Metcalf, was born July 17, 1921, near Helena, Alfalfa County, Oklahoma, and passed away March 12, 2009, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 87 years, 7 months, and 23 days. 	 Ivan graduated from Jet High School.  He was united in marriage to Beryl Florine Blackledge on July 28, 1943, at Coffeyville, Kansas. He entered the U.S. Army and was a Technician 4th Grade in the 46th Chemical Laboratory Company. After his discharge, they made their home near Jet where he farmed and ranched. 	 He was a member of the First Christian Church in Jet and Cherokee.  He enjoyed fishing, hunting, and gardening. 	 Besides his parents and wife, Beryl, Ivan was preceded in death by a set of stillborn twins; and two brothers, Dale and Earl Metcalf. 	 Ivan is survived by two sons, Verne Metcalf and his wife, Gloria, of Alva, and Kent Metcalf and his wife, Martha, of Perry; two brothers, Donald Metcalf and his wife, Florence, of Jet, and E.L. Metcalf and his wife, Betty, of Jet; one sister Jessie Mary Reinhart and her husband, Cecil, of Jet; and two sisters-in-law, Mabel Metcalf of Stillwater, and Francis Metcalf of Enid. 	 Also surviving are four grandchildren, Andrea Metcalf of Oklahoma City, Kristen Metcalf of San Antonio, Texas, and Ivan Metcalf and Sarah Metcalf, both of Perry; other relatives and friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Beadles Nursing Home Activity Fund.
