Funeral services for Deborah Marie Schwerdtfeger will be 1:00 p.m. Monday, September 13, 2010, at the Marshall Funeral Home Chapel. Interment will be in Capron Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home.  At her request, the casket will remained closed.  
Deborah Marie  "Deb? (Stallings) Schwerdtfeger, daughter of Dorthy Marie (Goss) Stallings and the late Louis Stallings, was born July 8, 1952, in Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away September 9, 2010, at Mercy Hospital in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, at the age of 58 years, 2 months, and 1 day.  
Deb graduated from Alva High School with the class of 1970, and attended Western Area Vo-Tech where she received her L.P.N. degree in 1976.  In 2002, she received her Bachelor of Arts degree in Health Care Administration from Almeda College and University.  On July 17, 1992, she was united in marriage to Gary Schwerdtfeger in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, where they made their home.  She was a Health Care Advocate with the Oklahoma Aging Advocacy.  
She enjoyed reading, entertaining her family and friends, and playing with her newborn grandson.  
She was preceded in death by her father.  
Deb is survived by her husband, Gary of Oklahoma City, her son, Curtis Diel, Jr. and his wife, Jennifer, of Oklahoma City; her mother, Dorthy Stallings of Alva;  two brothers, Mickey Stallings and his wife, Martha of Oklahoma City, and Phil Stallings and his wife, Clare, of Newton, Kansas; one grandson, Evan Diel of Oklahoma City.  
In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Oklahoma Lupus Association.
