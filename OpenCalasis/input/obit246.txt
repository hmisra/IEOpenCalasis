Funeral services for Illene Gulick will be 10:00 a.m. Wednesday, February 20, 2013, at the Ringwood Methodist Church with Rev. Terrill Parvin officiating.  Interment will be at Pleasant Ridge Cemetery southwest of Aline under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC, of Carmen.    
Amy Illene Gulick, daughter of the late Uel G. and Francis (Easter) Lemaster, was born December 25, 1924, in Jerico Springs, Missouri, and passed away February 13, 2013, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 88 years, 1 month, and 19 days.    
Illene graduated high school in Redondo Beach, California. On August 27, 1942, she was united in marriage to Walter Marrion Gulick in Yuma, California.  To this union two children were born, Terry and Joy. They made their home in Gardena, California.  A few years after the death of her husband in 1978, she moved to Oklahoma spending the last 10 years in Ringwood.     
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Marrion, and two brothers, Grant Lemaster and Les Lemaster.    
She is survived by one son, Terry Gulick and his wife, Sue, of Ringwood, one daughter, Joy Lawrence and her husband, Danny, of Fairview; four grandchildren, April Mueller, and her husband, David, Danny Gulick and his wife, Aranda, Jamie McCullough and her husband, Jason, and Erica Lawrence; nine great grandchildren, Ashley Mueller, Kaylee Mueller, Desiree Gulick, Amber Gulick, Blaine Gulick, Conner Gulick, Emily Mueller, Kason McCullough, and Colt McCullough; other relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be through the funeral home to the Ringwood Fire Department.
