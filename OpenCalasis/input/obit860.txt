Funeral Service for Leona Ingraham, will be 10:00 a.m., Saturday, September 15, 2012, at the Alva Church of God with Rev. Bob Brown officiating. Burial will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.   Leona Hazel Ingraham, daughter of the late George Henry and Olive Hazel  (Bush) Harper, was born August 30, 1923, at Quinlan, Oklahoma, and passed away September 12, 2012, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 89 years and13 days.    
She graduated from Quinlan High School with the Class of 1940.  February 1, 1941, she was united in marriage to Charles Mason Ingraham at Woodward.  They lived in Kiowa before moving to Alva in 1982.  
Leona was a member of the Alva Church of God Church.  She enjoyed sewing, reading, playing cards and crafts.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Charles, and one son, Kenneth Ingraham.  
Leona is survived by her son, Keith Ingraham and his wife, Sue, of Springfield, Missouri; her daughter, Charlene Campbell of Alva; one brother, Kenneth Harper of Quinlan; seven grandchildren, Steven Ingraham and wife, Mary, of Tuttle, Libby Brewer, and husband, Rodney, of Perryton, Texas; Bill Ingraham and wife, Lori, of Chickasha, Kathy Perry of Springfield, Missouri,  Cynthia Smith and husband, Gary, of McKinney, Texas, Karen Campbell of Alva, and David Ingraham and wife, Hope, of Alva,; 15 great grandchildren; 5 great great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Alva Church of God.
