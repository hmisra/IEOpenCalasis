Funeral services for Nelta Jane Coulter will be 10:30 a.m. Tuesday, September 16, 2008, at the Waynoka First Baptist Church with Rev. David Shaw officiating. Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka.  
Nelta Jane Coulter, daughter of the late Vernon R. And Ruth (Stevens) Hartley, was born March 9, 1920, at Cedardale, Oklahoma, and passed away, September 12, 2008, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 88 years, 6 months, and 3 days.  
Nelta attended Cedardale School. On July 9, 1938, she was united in marriage to Muriel Francis Coulter at Alva, Oklahoma. They lived their entire life in the Waynoka area. He preceded her in death on January 12, 1996.  
She was a member of the Waynoka First Baptist Church. She was also very active in the Waynoka Saddle Club for over 45 years and was always cooking for somebody all the time.  
Besides her parents and her husband, she was preceded in death by two brothers, Virgil Hartley and Eugene Hartley; and two sisters, Maxine Helton and Verna Goff.  
Nelta is survived by one son, Leo Coulter and wife, Rita, of Woodward; one daughter, Colene Green of Waynoka; and two sisters; Mildred Sampson of Waynoka, and Velma Sampson of Waynoka.  
Also surviving are five grandchildren, Greg Coulter and wife, Julie, of Woodward, Charity Coulter of Woodward, Mike Green and wife, Tisha, of Newton, Kansas, JoAnne Cox of Wichita, Kansas, and Brandon Green and wife, Felicia, of Waynoka; 14 great grandchildren; 2 great great grandchildren; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka Senior Citizen?s Center.
