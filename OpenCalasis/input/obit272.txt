Funeral services for Donna Lee Simmons will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, December 22, 2010, at the Marshall Funeral Home Chapel. Interment will be in the Memorial Hill Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Donna Lee Simmons, daughter of the late Leroy Dave and Grace America (Thompson) Settle, was born August 16, 1946, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away December 17, 2010, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 64 years, 4 months, and 1 day.  
Donna was in the last class to graduate from Byron-Driftwood High School, graduating in 1964.  She received her Early Childhood Degree in Education from Northwestern Oklahoma State University.  On December 23, 1967, she was united in marriage to Robert Jake Simmons at the Byron Christian Church.  She taught school in Warner Robbins School in Georgia, Tonkawa, and Cherokee before retiring in 2006 after teaching at Caney Valley School in Ramona, Oklahoma, for several years.  She did not want to retire because she loved working with the children so much.  
She was a member of the Byron Christian Church and the Oklahoma Education Association.  All of her life, she loved caring for animals.  She was known by all who knew her as a kind, loving, caring person.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Robert Simmons.  
Donna is survived by one son, Stephen Scott Simmons of Enid; two sisters, Sue and Karen Settle of Enid, Oklahoma; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Creature Concerns.
