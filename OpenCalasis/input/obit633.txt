Graveside services for Stuart Hodges will be 10:00 a.m. Saturday, May 25, 2013, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Don Hull officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.    
Stuart Hodges, son of the late Harold and Della (Howell) Hodges, was born June 15, 1920, in Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away May 23, 2013, in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, at the age of 92 years, 11 months, and 8 days.    
Stuart graduated from Waynoka High School.  On January 16, 1960, he was united in marriage to Eldena Boland Drake at Tucumcari, New Mexico.  They made their home in Waynoka where he worked as a switchman for the railroad, an oilfield pumper, and as a farmer/rancher.    
He was a member of the Morning Star Baptist Church.  He enjoyed horses and visiting with family and friends.    
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by his wife, Eldena; and one grandson, Darin Clapper.    
Stuart is survived by four children, Rex Hodges and wife, Winona, of Woodward, LeaAnn Miller and husband, Allen, of Waynoka, Tama Weitfeldt and husband, Tim, of Denver, Colorado, and Mac Drake and wife, Linda, of Waynoka; and one sister, Waynona Jones of Winfield, Kansas.    
Also surviving are seven grandchildren, John Miller and wife, LeAnne, of Wichita, Kansas, Mylee Bell and husband, Jon, of Amarillo, Texas, Kama Brune and husband, Phillip, of Joplin, Missouri, Melanie Green and husband, Danny, of Waynoka, Kellie Taylor and husband, Kyle, of Woodward, and Mindy Holt and husband, J. T. of Waynoka; 13 great grandchildren; 1 great great grandchild; other relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka EMTs.
