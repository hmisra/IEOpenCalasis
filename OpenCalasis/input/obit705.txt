Funeral services for Florence Ging will be 11:00 a.m. Tuesday, July 6, 2010, at the Wentworth Chapel with Rev. Ron Olson officiating. Interment will be in the Carmen City Cemetery under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC, Carmen. 	 Florence Adaline (Razey) Ging, daughter of the late George and Elvira Elizabeth (Lee) Razey, was born the youngest of seven children on June 3, 1919, on a farm near Carmen, Oklahoma, and passed away on July 2, 2010, at Yukon, Oklahoma, at the age of 91 years and 29 days. 	 Florence grew up and attended school in Carmen. On September 18, 1935, she was united in marriage to Ervin E. Ging at Hopeton, Oklahoma. She worked as a janitor for the school system and later worked at the Alfalfa County Sheriff?s Office. Florence was living in Cherokee when her husband passed away in 2003. After his death she moved to Yukon for a couple of years and then to Enid for the past three years. 	 Besides her parents and her husband, she was preceded in death by her infant daughter, Theona Ging, her son, Ernest Ging, two brothers, and four sisters. 	 Florence is survived by her grandchildren, Linda Shawn of Yukon, Danny Ging of Oklahoma City, and Jerry Ging of Bethany; four great grandchildren; one great great grandchild; other relatives and friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to March of Dimes.
