Funeral services for Wilma Jane Simmons will be 10:00 a.m. Thursday, December 20, 2007 at Schooler Funeral Home, Brentwood Chapel, in Amarillo with Rev. G. A. Roach officiating.  Graveside service will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, December 21, 2007 at Carmen Cemetery in Carmen Oklahoma.  Interment is under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC.  
Wilma Jane Simmons, daughter of the late John and Ethel Barnett, was born April 11, 1926 in Del Rio, Texas, and passed away December 15, 2007 in Amarillo, Texas, at the age of 81 years, 8 months and 4 days.  
On November 11, 1944, she was united in marriage to Corwin Simmons.  As a lifelong caregiver she worked as a Nurse???s Aide at Bivins Nursing Home for 27 years.  She loved taking pictures and playing with her grandchildren and great grandchildren.  
She was preceded in death by her husband, Corwin; an infant son, David; and three brothers, Dan, Jimmy, and Raymond Barnett.  
Wilma is survived by one daughter, Carolyn Arnold of Amarillo; two sons, Kenneth Simmons of Amarillo, and John Simmons of Amarillo; one sister, Gladys Ethel Green of San Antonio, Texas; 9 grandchildren; 21 great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.
