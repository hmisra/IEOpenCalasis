NOTE:  Time of service has been changed to 11:00 a.m, Monday, due to lock down of Ft. Sill Army Base because of weather conditions.  
 Funeral services for Derrick Dean Phillips will be 11:00 a.m. Monday, February 1, 2010, at the Waynoka Church of God with a United States Chaplain officiating. Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Derrick Dean Phillips, son of Verl Phillips and the late Sheila Kay Phillips, was born January 20, 1983, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away January 23, 2010, at Stillwater, Oklahoma, at the age of 27 years and 3 days.  
Derrick attended school at Waynoka before receiving his GED. He enlisted in the United States Army where he served five years, including one year in Iraq. On December 20, 2004, he was united in marriage to Jesica Nicole Raymond at Applevalley, California. To this union two children were born, Gage and Alexandra.  
He was very mechanically inclined and loved working on cars. He also enjoyed camping and fishing.  
He was preceded in death by his mother, Sheila Phillips.  
Derrick is survived by his wife, Jesica, of Haughton, Louisiana; his two children, Gage and Alexandra Phillips of Haughton, Louisiana; his father, Verl Phillips and fianc?, Audra, of Waynoka; his grandmother, LaEtta Phillips of Waynoka; four sisters, Starla Phillips of Waynoka, Michella Cowan of Waynoka, Angelica Johnston of Waynoka, and Cherina Cagwin of Coyle; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to his grandmother.
