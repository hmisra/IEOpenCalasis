Funeral services for Robert (Bob) William Trekell, Jr. will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, April 16, 2008, at the Alva Friends Church with Pastor Mark McDonald, officiating.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva. 	 Robert William Trekell, Jr., son of Robert, Sr. and Faye Elaine (Hada) Trekell, was born July 1, 1954, Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away April 12, 2008, at Share Medical Center, Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 53 years, 9 months, and 10 days. 	 Bob graduated from Alva High School and Northwestern State University and also attended school at the University of Texas in Austin.  He was a double line operator for Jarvis Construction Company and later worked under his brother, Kenneth, at Beredco Drilling Company.  He was currently employed in the meat department at WalMart. 	 Growing up as a Boy Scout, Bob earned many awards including, Order of the Arrow, God and Country, Mile Swim, and the highest honor as Eagle Scout.  He became a scout master and taught Sunday School at the Alva Friends Church.  Bob was an avid reader, enjoyed his computer, and loved the outdoors. 	 He was preceded in death by his grandparents, Kalman and Faye Hada, and Harvey and Jesse Trekell; and his brother, Kenneth Trekell, Sr. 	 Bob is survived by his parents, Robert and Elaine Trekell, Sr.; two sisters, La Vonna Gribble of Alva, and Judy Kirby and her husband, Ron, of Lawton, Oklahoma; aunts, uncles, nieces and nephews; other relatives and friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Alva Friends Church, Boy Scouts of America, or the American Heart Association.
