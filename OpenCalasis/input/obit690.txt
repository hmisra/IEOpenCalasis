Howard Dannar, son of the late Everette Glen and Goldie Violet (Waite) Dannar, was born June 7, 1927, and passed away June 3, 2006, at Integris Bass Hospital in Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 78 years, 11 months and 28 days.  Mr. Dannar?s wish was to be cremated and his ashes buried at his home.  A memorial service will be held at a later date.  
On March 10, 1972, he was united in marriage to Irene M. Huhs at Oskloosa, Kansas.  He worked as in independent drywall construction contractor.  
He enjoyed fishing and playing pool.  He especially enjoyed his kids and grandchildren.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by his first wife, Alta Parker Dannar; and 2 brothers Earl Dannar and Leroy Dannar.  
Howard is survived by his wife, "Sam", of Waynoka; two sons, Lynn Dannar and his wife, Sharon, of Topeka, Kansas, and Alan Dannar and his wife, Judy, of Optima, Oklahoma; one daughter, Diana Lynn Parsel of Perry, Kansas; two brothers, Harold Dannar of Pawnee, Oklahoma and Junior Lee Dannar of Waynoka; two sisters, Virginia Mae Lovette of Topeka, Kansas, and Lou Mae Rose of Austin, Texas; one half sister, Elsie of Woodward; 6 grandchildren; 11 great grandchildren; 1 great great grandson; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the chariey of your choice.
