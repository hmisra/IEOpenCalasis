Mass of Christian Burial for Samuel (Antonio) Vargas Celalla will be 11:00 a.m. Friday, September 2, 2011, at Our Mother of Mercy Church in Waynoka with Father James Wickersham serving as the celebrant.  Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka.  A rosary will be held at 5:00 p.m., Thursday, September 1, 2011, at the funeral home.  
Samuel (Antonio) Vargas Celalla, son of Cornelio Vargas and Maria Celaya (Gonzales) Perez, was born November 5, 1953, in Agua Calienta, Durango, Mexico, and passed away August 31, 2011, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 57 years, 9 months, and 26 days.  
Antonio attended school in Mexico.  On July 2, 1988, he was united in marriage to Silvia Cruz Gallo, at Durango, Mexico.  They lived in Juarez, Chihuahua, Mexico until they moved to Waynoka in 1993.  He worked at CDR until it closed.  He also mowed lawns and was a handyman for anyone who needed him.  
Antonio is survived by his wife, Silvia, of Waynoka; three sons, Hugo, Jorge, and Julio Vargas; and five daughters, Dora, Norma, Concha, Jannette, and Lupita Vargas.  
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
