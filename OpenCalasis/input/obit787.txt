Memorial services for Gary Lynn Zook will be 12:00 p.m. Wednesday, December 16, 2009, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Rev. David Shaw officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Gary Lynn Zook, son of the late James and Verda (Grant) Zook, was born September 9, 1946 in Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away December 12, 2009, at Wichita Falls, Texas, at the age of 63 years, 3 months, and 3 days.  
After Gary graduated from Waynoka High School he served his country in the United States Army.  He advanced to the rank of Sergeant.  He made his home in Waynoka where he was employed by Woods County.  
Gary was a member of Disabled American Veterans.  He enjoyed hunting and fishing as well as tinkering in the garage, especially on small engines.  He was also an avid reader of western novels.  
Gary is survived by one son, Kenneth James Zook of Harrold, Texas; one daughter, Sherri Lynn Clouse and husband, Timothy, of Harrold, Texas; one brother, Richard Zook and wife, Ava, of Waynoka; one sister, Rosa Lee Whipple and husband, Dennis, of Waynoka; four grandchilren, Jacob Brown, Cole Clouse, Colton Clouse, and Terry Charles Clouse, all of Harrold, Texas; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Share Hospice.
