Gladys Ellen Rose daughter of the late Albert Nathan Rose and Revenna Luella (Arndt) Rose was born May 18, 1923, northwest of Alva on the farm and passed away July 8, 2006, in Alva, Oklahoma at the age of 83 years, 1 month, and 20 days. 	 Gladys attended school at Whitehorse and graduated from Farry High School in 1942.  After graduating Gladys taught at Lookout and Weber  Schools for four years.  She also worked as a telephone operator from 1960 to 1963 and for Ferguson&amp;s Laundry.  She worked for Share Convalescent Home in the Housekeeping Department for thirteen years until her retirement.  She was Employee of the Year in 1989 	 Gladys loved the outdoors and she enjoyed gardening and taking care of her chickens.  She also loved to crochet, embroidery, and play bingo. 	 Besides her parents Gladys was preceded in death by her brother, Albert L.  "Bud" Rose and an infant sister, Alma Viola Rose. 	 She is survived by her sister, Iva Jean Rose, of Enid; four nephews, Leonard Max Rose and his wife, Mary, of Alva, DeWayne Lee Rose and his wife , Kathy, of Caney, Kansas, Lester Gene Rose and his wife, Nancy, of Wichita, Kansas, and Keith Allen Rose and his wife, Brenda, of Alva.  She is also survived by several great nieces and nephews and great great nieces and nephews. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for cancer research.
