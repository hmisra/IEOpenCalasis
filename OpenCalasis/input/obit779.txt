Funeral services for Gary Dean Maul, will be 2:00 p.m. Saturday, September 10, 2011, at the Dacoma Church of God with Rev. Connie Kinnard officiating.  Interment will be in the Union Center Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Gary Dean Maul, son of Milford Wayne and the late Carol Joyce (Lindsley) Maul, was born February 2, 1966, at Longmont, Colorado, and passed away September 7, 2011, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 45 years 7 months and 5 days.  
Gary graduated from Carman-Dacoma High School with the class of 1984. In 1994 he was united in marriage to Betty (Nuckols) Panick at Dacoma.  They made their home in Dacoma.   Gary attended the Hopeton Wesleyan church.  He was a carpenter and handyman. He won the hot pepper contest in Alva and enjoyed working on old cars and loved to paint.  
Besides his mother, Gary was preceded in death by his wife, Betty, and one brother, Allen Maul.	  
Gary is survived by his father and step-mother, Milford and Sharon Maul, one step son, Daniel Panick of Guthrie, one daughter, Jo Lynn Manion and her husband, Eric, of Lebanon, Oklahoma, two brothers, Mike Maul and his wife, Traci, of Dacoma and John Maul, also of Dacoma; one granddaughter, Iris Manion, of Lebanon; nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
