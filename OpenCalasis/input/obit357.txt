Funeral services for Betty Jean Replogle will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, April 4, 2007, at the Waynoka Church of God with Pastor John Brint officiating.  Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Betty Jean Replogle, daughter of the late Ted Alex and Mabel Ruth (Janney) Walker, was born March 31, 1938 at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away April 1, 2007, at Edmond Regional Medical Center, Edmond, Oklahoma, at the age of 69 years and one day.  
She spent most of her life in Waynoka and graduated from Waynoka High School. Betty was united in marriage to Donald Replogle on June 13, 1959 at the Waynoka Church of God.  To this union, three children were born: Alice, Debbie, and Ted.   
Betty was a member of the Waynoka Church of God.  She was a homemaker and an avid collector of recipe books.  She also enjoyed working puzzles and sewing.  
She was preceded in death by her parents.  
Betty is survived by her husband, Donald Replogle, of the home; her children, Alice Stratton and her husband, Jerry, of Edmond, Debbie McWhirter and her husband, Carl, of New Caney, Texas, and Ted Replogle and his wife, Stacey, of Waynoka.  She is also survived by seven grandchildren, Colby Stratton and Kelsey Stratton, of Edmond, Lauren McWhirter, Tim McWhirter, and Michael McWhirter, of New Caney, Texas, Stephen Replogle and Justin Replogle, of Waynoka.  
Also surviving are her brother, Jack Walker and his wife, Pat, of Waynoka, and her sister, Bonita Gelhaar and her husband, Mark, of Lumberton, Mississippi; other relatives and friends.  
Memorials may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka E.M.T.?s.
