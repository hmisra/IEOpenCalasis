A memorial service for Nova Dean “Nick” Burton, Jr., will be 2:00 pm  Sunday, November 30, 2014, at the Marshall Funeral Home Chapel.  Nick was cremated at his request under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  	    
Nova Dean “Nick” Burton, Jr., son of the late Nova “Dean” Burton and the late  Darlene Bell (Savely) Burton, was born August 24, 1960, in Bethesda, Maryland, and passed away November 25, 2014, at Freedom, Oklahoma, at the age of 54 years, 3 months, and 1 day.   	    
Nick graduated from high school in Pueblo, Colorado, with the Class of 1978.  He then attended Colorado Aero Tech and worked as an airplane mechanic.  He later was a car salesman.  He lived in the Denver/Pueblo area until 1999 when he moved to Freedom.  	    
Nick attended the Freedom Christian Church.  Nick grew up in the outdoors and enjoyed hunting and fishing.  When he was younger, he enjoyed Motocross.  He enjoyed watching NASCAR on television and loved the Dallas Cowboys Football Team.  	    
Nick is survived by his sister, Belinda Burton-Garinger of Freedom; his step-mother, Linda Burton of Pueblo, Colorado; two uncles, Ottie Savely of Freedom and Bill Savely of Freedom.  	    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Freedom Ambulance Service.
