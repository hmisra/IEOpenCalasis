Graveside services for Elaine Boyle Gillison will be 10:00 a.m. Monday, May 7, 2007, at the Waynoka Cemetery with Reverend John Brint, pastor of the Waynoka Church of God officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Elaine Boyle Gillison, daughter of the late William LeRoy and Pauline Palistine (Highfill) Boyle, was born September 11, 1920, in Mooreland, Oklahoma, and passed away May 4, 2007, at the Waynoka Nursing Center in Waynoka, Oklahoma, at the age of 86 years, 7 months, and 23 days.  
Elaine graduated from Waynoka High School and attended college in Oklahoma City.  She lived in Albuquerque, New Mexico before moving to Waynoka 18 years ago.  
She was a member of the Baptist Church.  Her children were always the most important aspect of her life.  She also enjoyed reading, working crossword puzzles, and was an excellent cook.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by one daughter, Sarah Gillison; one brother, Robert Boyle; and one sister, Nadine Vanderslice.  
Elaine is survived by one son, William Robert Gillison and his wife, Fran, of Los Alamos, New Mexico; two daughters, Jeannie Glommen and her husband, Dan, of Winston, Georgia, and Paula Tatar and her husband, Dan, of Glen Allen, Virginia.  
Also surviving are five grandchildren, Gary Glommen and his wife, Cindy, of Georgia, Tracy O&amp;Barr and her husband, David, of Georgia, Julie Groom and her husband, Todd, of Georgia, Sally Gillison Schlather and her husband, Erick, of San Antonio, Texas, and Dr. Aimee Gillison Henley and her husband, Ryan, of Denver, Colorado; ten great grandchildren, Daniel O&amp;Barr, Cole Glommen, Brian Groom, Jenna Groom, Gracie Groom, Heather Groom, William Andrew Schlather, Elizabeth Grey Schlather, Arden Scott Henley, and William Ryan Henley; other relatives and friends.
