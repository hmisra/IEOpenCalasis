Celebration of life services for Kenneth Joseph “Joe” Brown will be 2:00 p.m. Monday, February 9, 2015, at the Hopeton Wesleyan Church with Pastor Marion Hadsell officiating.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  	    
There will be a visitation with the family at the home of his grandfather, Kenneth H. Brown from 5:00 to 7:00 p.m. Sunday.  	    
Kenneth Joseph “Joe” Brown, son of Kenneth David Brown and Cheryl Ann Hardiman, was born May 30, 1996, at Enid, Oklahoma, and passed away February 1, 2015, at Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, at the age of 18 years, 8 months, and 1 day.  	    
Joe attended school in Alva, Oklahoma, and Goddard, Kansas.  He was an amateur boxer, loved music, his family, documentaries, and looked forward to learning to weld.  	    
He was preceded in death by his paternal grandmother, Dale Brown.  	    
Joe is survived by his mother, Cheryl Brown of Alva; his father, Kenneth David Brown and wife, Carrie, of Goddard, Kansas; two sisters, Kaytlin Brown of Guthrie and Taylor Brown of Goddard; his grandparents, Kenneth H. Brown of Alva, Elvin and Mary Hardiman of St. Joseph, Missouri, Janet M. Hardiman of Anthony, Kansas, and Lawrence and Sherryl Doud of Aurora, Missouri; other relatives and friends.  	    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to establish a scholarship for his sister, Kaytlin, or the United Way.  	    
Remembrances may be shared with the family at www.marshallfuneralhomes.com.
