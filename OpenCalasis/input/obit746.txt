Graveside services for Geneva Maxine Munger will be 10:00 a.m. Wednesday, December 1, 2010, at the Alva Municipal Cemetery with Kevin Copenhaver officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Geneva Maxine Munger, daughter of the late William "Bill? and Flora (Scott) Fish, was born December 3, 1919, in Winchester, Woods County, Oklahoma, and passed away November 28, 2010, at Kinsley, Kansas.  
Geneva graduated from Farry High School.  On July 14, 1940, she was united in marriage to Harold Glen Munger at the Cedar Grove Wesleyan Parsonage northwest of Alva.  They lived in Alva where she attended the Alva Church of God.  They moved to Enid where they attended the Enid Maine Church of the Nazarene.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Harold.  
Geneva is survived by three sons, Harold Glade Munger of Lake Havasu City, Arizona, Doyle Munger and wife, Anita, of Enid, and Alvin Munger and wife, Brenda, of Kinsley, Kansas; two daughters, Marguerite Copenhaver and husband, Dean, of Enid, and Glenda Schumacher and husband, Von, of Enid; one brother, Lyle Wayne Fish and wife, Ruth, of Oregon; one sister, Eva Collie of Wichita; 9 grandchildren, 13 great grandchildren; 2 great great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Gideon Bible Society.
