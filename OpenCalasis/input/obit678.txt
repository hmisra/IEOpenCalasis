Graveside services for Lonnie Eugene "Bud? Bolar will be 2:00 p.m. Saturday, September 27, 2008, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Rev. David Shaw, pastor of the Waynoka First Baptist Church officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  At Bud?s request, the casket will remain closed.  
Lonnie Eugene "Bud? Bolar, son of the late Albert Eugene and Nanny Nevella (Miller) Bolar, was born April 3, 1944, and passed away September 24, 2008, at the Waynoka Nursing Center in Waynoka, Oklahoma, at the age of 64 years, 5 months, and 21 days.  
Bud graduated from Waynoka High School with the Class of 1963.  Lonnie joined the United States Air Force on November 16, 1965, and served as an Air Policeman for a total of 7 years.  While stationed in Viet Nam, his base came under attack by the enemy forces and the base was held.  For his role in this attack he was awarded a medal from his Commander, along with other fellow soldiers.  He was stationed at Tan Su Nuet Air Base.  Bud received an honorable discharge and was at the rank of Sergeant when he left the military.  
Bud moved back to his home town of Waynoka and ventured into barber college in Oklahoma City, and then into law enforcement.  Bud?s career was cut short due to his medical condition in the mid to late 70&amp;s.  
He has lived in Waynoka most of his adult life, and his favorite past time was fishing.  He loved to fish at friend?s ponds around Waynoka.  He was a member of the First Baptist Church in Waynoka.   
Bud is survived by his daughter, Teresa Bolar of Iowa; one brother, Donald "Duck? Bolar and his wife, Brenda, of Follett, Texas; two sisters, Ladonna Whitney and husband, Lowell, of Waynoka, and Twana Howard and husband, Donald, of, Selman, Oklahoma; other relatives and many friends.  He was loved and will be missed by many.  
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
