Graveside services for Steve Kimbro will be 10:00 a.m. Friday, January 22,  2010, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Reverend Charles Newton officiating.  Interment will follow in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Stephen Martell Kimbro, son of Martha Lee (Hoover) Kimbro and the late Omer De Fay "Brownie? Kimbro, was born July 19, 1952, in Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away January 18, 2010, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 57 years, 5 months, and 30 days.  
Steve graduated from Waynoka High School in 1969.  He worked for Halliburton as a cementer.  Steve married Connie Jean Atwell at Woodward.  They had one son, Tracy Leon Kimbro.  They lived in Woodward and then Waynoka for several years.  
He was a member of the Baptist Church.  He loved  hunting and fishing.  He, also, loved making things with his hands and enjoyed woodworking and painting with water colors.    
Steve was preceded in death by his father, Brownie.  
He is survived by his son, Tracy Kimbro, of Woodward; his mother, Martha Kimbro of Waynoka; his twin sister, Claudia Steffen, of Tulsa; and one brother, Kim Kimbro and wife, Deb, of Waynoka; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
