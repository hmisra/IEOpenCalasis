Funeral services for LaDonna Rae Fuller will be 12:00 p.m. Thursday, January 3, 2013, at the Marshall Funeral Home Chapel, with Rev. John Smith, officiating.  Interment will be 2:00 p.m. Thursday in the Capron Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
LaDonna Rae Fuller, daughter of Mary Deneice Rucker and the late Daniel Wayne Lynch, was born March 3, 1968, at Wellington, Kansas, and passed away December 23, 2012, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 44 years, 9 months, and 20 days.    
LaDonna attended school in Cherokee, Alva, and Enid.  In 1984, she was united in marriage to Thomas Fredrick Fuller at Oklahoma City.  She made her home in Alva where she worked cleaning nursing home and motel rooms, as a lab tech, and as a CNA.    
She was a member of the Sacred Heart Church and attended the College Hill Church of Christ.  She enjoyed helping people, swimming, and loved playing with her grandkids.    
Besides her father, she was preceded in death by her son, Joshua Fuller.    
LaDonna is survived by her companion, Scott Rankin; her son, Jeremy Fuller and wife, Jonette, of Hartshorne; her mother, Dee Rucker of Alva; her brother, Derrick Dirham of Oklahoma City; her sister, Lora Dirham of Enid; three grandchildren, Drake Thomas, Joshua Dash, and Nathaniel Fox; and her aunts and uncles, Susan Yonemura, Dory Reader, Monty Dirham, and Tana Dirham; other relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
