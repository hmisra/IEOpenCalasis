Marguerite Marie Moser, 90, of Edmond,  Oklahoma, was born December 08, 1919, near Cherokee, Oklahoma, to William and Anna Marie (Patzkowsky) Schwindt.  She died June 28, 2010, in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.  Marguerite married Dean Moser in 1941 at Cherokee. She was a dietician for the Barber County Kansas School District for 19 years.  She is survived by her son, Kelly Moser of Edmond, and a grandson, Kelly Dean Moser and his wife, Wendy, of Edmond. Marguerite was preceded in death by her husband, Dean, two brothers and four sisters. She was cremated at her request with no services. Arrangements are with Wentworth Mortuary, LLC.
