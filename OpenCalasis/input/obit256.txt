Funeral services for Anna Mae Dolasinski Ward of Carmen, Oklahoma, will be 10:30 a.m. Monday, August 16, 2010, at the St. Cornelius Catholic Church in Cherokee with Father Lawrence officiating.  Interment will be in the Aline Star Cemetery under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC of Carmen. 	 Anna Mae Ward, daughter of the late Charles and Irene T. (Goss) Thatcher, was born May 1, 1922, at Knoxville, Iowa, and passed away at her home in Carmen, Oklahoma, August 12, 2010, at the age of 88 years, 3 months, and 11 days. 	 Anna Mae attended school and graduated June, 1940, in Gary, Indiana.  On November 14, 1942, she was united in marriage to Zeno Dolasinski at Gary, Indiana.  To this union one daughter and three sons were born: Toni, Allan, Gary, and Mark.  After the death of her husband, she moved her family to Carmen. 	 Anna Mae was a devoted member of the St. Cornelius Catholic Church.  She enjoyed playing bingo, crocheting and quilting.  She also volunteered for the voter registration and election board. 	 Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Zeno; a brother, Charles Thatcher, Jr.; and two sisters, Jerry Snook and Phyllis Ward. 	 Anna Mae is survived by her daughter, Toni Best and her husband, Ed, of Enid, her sons, Allan Dolasinski and wife, Harriet, of Carmen, Gary Dolasinski and wife, Vicki Lynn, of Carmen, Mark Dolasinski of Enid; five grandchildren, Darla Morvant of Enid, Thomas Graham of Enid, Doreen Ward and husband, Rob, of Aline, Edward Best, II of Lahoma, and Tracey Bowers and husband, Tim, of Enid; eight great grandchildren; five great great grandchildren; other relatives and friends. 	 Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Share Hospice.
