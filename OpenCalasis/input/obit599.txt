Interment for Mary Ruth Beadles Hiett will be 9:30 a.m. Tuesday, April 2, 2013, at the Alva Municipal Cemetery.  Memorial services will follow at 11:00 a.m. at the Alva Church of the Nazarene with Rev. Bill Kalfas officiating.  Services are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Mary Ruth Hiett, daughter of the late William Thomas and Bessie Margaret (Lewis) Beadles, was born November 25, 1918, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away March 27, 2013, at Beadles Nursing Home in Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 94 years, 4 months, and 2 days.    
Ruth graduated from Alva High School and furthered her education at Northwestern State College and at Woodward where she earned her LPN.  On November 28, 1936, she was united in marriage to Charley Franklin Hiett at Enid, Oklahoma.  They made their home in Alva where her family founded, owned and operated Beadles Nursing Home where she served as administrator for many years.  She also worked as an administrator at Share Convalescent Home for several years.    
She was a lifetime member of the Alva Church of the Nazarene. Ruth served on the church board and as church Treasurer for many years, taught junior high, high school and adult Sunday school classes along with directing Vacation Bible School for many years. She loved memorizing Scripture and enjoyed fishing, traveling, and camping. Ruth also loved to embroidery and made many lovely quilts for family and friends.    
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Charley Hiett; two sons, Marion Hiett and infant Billy Dean Hiett; one daughter Emma Jordan; several brothers and sisters, including Joe Beadles, Lewis Beadles, Edith Wheeler and Gussie Rader; two grandchildren, Janice Terres and Robbie Lynch; two great grandchildren, Amber and Ashley Huckabee; and a niece who was like a sister, Thelma Riley.
