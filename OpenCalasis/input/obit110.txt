Graveside services for Darrell Gene Miller will be 1:00 p.m. Tuesday, January 13, 2015, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with William Simon officiating.  Interment will follow in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.	    
Darrell Gene Miller, son of the late Alva and Naomi (McCully) Miller was born November 24, 1935, in Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away in Stillwater, Oklahoma, January 10, 2015 at the age of 79 Years, 1 month, and 17 Days.    
	  Darrell lived in Waynoka his entire life until May of 2014 when he moved to Stillwater to live with family who could care for him after the death of his younger sister, Rita Inman and her husband Gregg Inman.    
	  He was adored by the residents of Waynoka and could be found most days taking his daily walks around the block. He was best known for his friendliness and big wave to all his friends.  Darrell was loved by many and will be greatly missed by all who knew him.   One of his favorite things to do was watch anything on the western channel with his good friend, Rick Wilbanks, who helped the family care for him.  	  When he moved to Stillwater, he always made sure people knew he was from Waynoka, Oklahoma. He was so proud of where he came from and had a great love for his family. He brought great joy to their lives.    
	  Besides his parents, Darrell was preceded in death by four brothers, Ray Dean, Dwayne, David, and Jimmy Miller; one sister, Rita Inman and her husband, Gregg Inman; two nieces, Stephanie Inman and Kathy (Kysar) Taylor.    
	  He is survived by one sister, Oleta Kysar and her husband, Sammy, of Oklahoma City; nieces, nephews, other relatives and friends.
