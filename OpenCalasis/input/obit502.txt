Funeral services for Roberta Ann Petermann will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, January 25, 2011, at the Zion Lutheran Church with Rev. Mark Bersche officiating.  Interment will be in the Zion Lutheran Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Roberta Ann Petermann, daughter of the late Robert and Virginia (Link) Trice, was born April 3, 1936, in Clarksville, Tennessee, and passed away January 20, 2011, at Edmond, Oklahoma, at the age of 74 years, 9 months, and 17 days.  
Roberta graduated from Boettcher School in Denver, Colorado and attended Colorado Women?s College for two years. She worked in a physician?s office for seven years while living in Denver. On March 13, 1965, she was united in marriage to Donald Petermann in Denver, Colorado. They made their home in Alva, Oklahoma. They established Alva Vet Supply in 1971. Donald preceded her in death on July 14, 1998. Roberta married Don Dallam in 2003 were they made their home in Oklahoma City. Don preceded her in death March of 2007.  
She was an active member of the Zion Lutheran Church. She served as president of the Alva Business and Professional Women, president of the Alva PTO, and was active in the Woods County Democratic Women.  
Roberta is survived by two daughters, Kristi Chappell and husband, Jack, of Edmond, Oklahoma, and Misti Petermann of Enid, Oklahoma; three grandchildren, Jacklyn and Andrew Chappell of Edmond and Landon Petermann-Norton of Noble; two sisters-in-law, Esther McIver and husband, Corky, of Alva, and Margaret Goll and husband, Marvin, of Enid; nieces, nephews, other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Zion Lutheran Church.
