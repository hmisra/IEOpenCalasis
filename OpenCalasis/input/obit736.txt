Funeral services for Bruce Meredith Perry will be 2:30 p.m. Monday, May 12, 2008, at the Marshall Funeral Home Chapel with Rev. Judye Pistole officiating.  There will be a visitation with the family at 1:00 p.m. prior to the service.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home.  
Bruce Perry, son of Carolyn (Caldwell) and the late Jimmy Jordan Perry, was born November 30, 1959, at Memphis, Tennessee, and passed away May 5, 2008, at St. Francis Health System, Tulsa, Oklahoma, at the age of 48 years, 5 months, and 5 days.  
Bruce graduated from Alva High School and was associated with the tire business all his life.  He was active in the family business, Perry Tire and Alignment in Alva.  Bruce was an avid golfer and enjoyed being with friends on the golf course.  One of his great loves was flying.  
He was preceded in death by his father, Jim.  
Bruce is survived by two daughters, Brooke Jordan Perry and Baylie Meredith Perry of Liberty, Missouri; his mother, Carolyn Perry of Lakeland, Florida; one brother Brent Perry, and his wife, Mendee, of Tulsa; and one sister; Beth Diacon of Lakeland, Florida; one niece, Britni Diacon of Lakeland, Florida .  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Honduras Mission of the All Saint?s Episcopal Church.
