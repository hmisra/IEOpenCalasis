Gary Lynn Jacobson was born October 7, 1963,  to Harry Newton and Mavis (Chipman) Jacobson at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away November 8, 2013, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 50 years, one month, and one day.    
Gary graduated from Waynoka High School.  He was a farmer and rancher  and lived his whole life on his farm near Waynoka.  Gary enjoyed hunting and fishing.    
Preceding Gary in death are his parents, Harry and Mavis, and a brother, Paul Jacobson.    
Gary is survived by relatives and many close friends.    
It was his wish to be cremated and have no service.  Arrangements are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.    
Memorial contributions may be made in his memory through the funeral home to the Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for cancer research.    
Remembrances may be shared with the family at www.marshallfuneralhomes.com.
