
					
				Graveside services for Randy Ray Parker will be 1:00 p.m. Thursday, February 19, 2015, at the Alva Municipal Cemetery with Pastor John Clapp officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Randy Ray Parker, son of the late John and Orvella Doris (Plummer) Parker, was born February 24, 1952, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away February 15, 2015, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 62 years, 11 months, and 21 days.    
Randy attended school in Alva, and graduated from high school in Tulsa, and attended Community College in San Diego, California.  He served in the United States Army and the United States Navy.  On March 26, 1975, he was united in marriage to Un Ye Mun in Seoul, Korea.  They made their home in Alva where he worked for Southern Star.    
He enjoyed antiques and collectibles, cooking, playing with his grandkids, and his dog, Penelope.    
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by his wife, Un Ye.    
Randy is survived by two daughters, Jana Lynn Parker of Alva, and Kimberly Marie Tucker and husband, Matt, of Cherokee; one sister, Doris Parker Grossman and husband, Dale, of California; two half brothers, R. J. Lieurance and Chad Lieurance, both of Alva; one half sister, Teresa Noyes, of Tulsa; seven grandchildren, Colby, Frances, Tierna, and Jacob Mustard, Kael Tucker, and Keyonna and Kayden Parker; other relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
