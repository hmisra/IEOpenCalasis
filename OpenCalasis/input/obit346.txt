Funeral services for Presley Lane Puffinbarger will be 10:00 a.m. Wednesday, September 2, 2009, at the Cherokee Friends Church with Rev. Sonny Daub officiating.  Interment will be in the Cherokee Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Presley Lane Puffinbarger, daughter of Cody and Kayla (Maltby) Puffinbarger, was born Thursday, August 27, 2009, at Mercy Hospital in Oklahoma City, and passed away Friday, August 28, 2009, at Mercy Hospital.  
Presley is survived by her parents, Cody and Kayla Puffinbarger of Cherokee; her brother and sister, Payton and Ashlyn; her grandparents, Bill and Elsie Maltby of Carmen, and Tommy and Angela Puffinbarger of Burlington; her great grandparents, Lynn and Winona Puffinbarger of Cherokee, and Ruth Whittle of Cherokee.
