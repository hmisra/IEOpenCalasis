Funeral services for Gladys Riley will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, April 22, 2011, at the Northside Church of Christ in Waynoka with Gene Goben officiating.  Interment will be in the Liberty Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC. Gladys Riley, daughter of the late William and Molly (Linder) Harmon, was born December 2, 1908, at Sherman, Oklahoma, and passed away April 19, 2011, at Woodward, Oklahoma, at the age of 102 years, 4 months, and 17 days.  
On December 6, 1927, Gladys was united in marriage to James Walker, who passed away May 16, 1951.  To this union five children were born.  On June 14, 1954, she was united in marriage to Arthur Leland Riley, who passed away March 9, 1975.  
She was a member of the Northside Church of Christ.  She enjoyed quillting and crocheting, and word search puzzles.  
She was preceded in death by all of her brothers and sisters; one grandson and one great granddaughter.  
Gladys is survived by her children, Wilma Hutchison of Waynoka, Donald Walker and wife, Patricia, of Chester, Orville Walker and wife, Barbara of Enid, Imojean Keller and husband, Gary of Woodward, and Patricia Jamison and husband, Dave of Woodward; 9 grandchildren; 16 great grandchildren; 7 great great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for Diabetes Research or the Gideons.
