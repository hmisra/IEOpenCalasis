Funeral services for Cecil Ray Hutton will be 10:00 a.m. Tuesday, October 12, 2010, at the Alva Church of the Nazarene with Rev. William Kalfas officiating.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva. The casket will be closed at the church.  
Cecil Ray Hutton, son of the late Harold Lee and Fredrica Lane (Dorsey) Hutton, was born December 25, 1954, at Albuquerque, New Mexico, and passed away October 7, 2010, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 55 years, 9 months, and 12 days.  
Since Cecil?s father was in the Air Force, Cecil attended several schools before graduating from Enid High School.  On January 4, 1985, he was united in marriage to Ellen Whitcomb at Wellington, Kansas.  They lived in Alva where he was employed by Alva Concrete.  A short time later he went to work for the City of Alva where he supervised a work crew from BJCC on various projects around the city.  
Cecil attended the Alva Church of the Nazarene and the Alva Moose Lodge.  He enjoyed woodworking, yard work, and collecting pocket knives and John Wayne memorabilia.  He always helped other people in any way he could.  
He was preceded in death by his parents.  
Cecil is survived by his wife, Ellen, of Alva; three daughters, Delanie Rich of Richmond, Kentucky, Raven Whitcomb of Alva, and Pattie Liggenstoffer and husband, Buddy, of Tulsa; one brother, Harold "Dee? Hutton and wife, Carrie, of Denver, Colorado; one sister, Pat Hutton of Tulsa; one granddaughter, Lydia Berry; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made to the funeral home to assist with funeral expenses or to the American Cancer Society.
