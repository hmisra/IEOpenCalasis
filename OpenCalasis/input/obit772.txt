Alena Annette Parson, daughter of Annabelle Lee (Snyder) Parson and the late Norman Lee Parson, was born September 14, 1947, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away December 24, 2007, at her home in Alva, at the age of 60 years, 3 months, and 9 days.  
After Alena graduated from Dacoma High School she attended Beauty College in Enid.  She also took several photography classes in Fayetteville, Arkansas.  She was a professional photographer who immensely enjoyed her work.  She lived all over the United States before returning to Alva five years ago, but she always considered Slidell, Louisiana, to be home.  
She was a member of the Green Valley United Methodist Church.  She loved animals, especially horses.  She also enjoyed helping people.  
She was preceded in death by her father, Norman.  
Alena is survived by her mother, Annabelle, of Alva; one daughter, Melissa Morales and her husband Noah, of Cave Creek, Arizona; two sisters, Kathy Spears and her husband, Jim, of Oklahoma City, and Kim Treas and her husband, Barry, of Sulphur, Louisiana; two step children, Danny and Kelly; one step grandson, Ethan; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to New Beginnings.
