Memorial services for Douglas Grounds will be 1:00 p.m., Saturday, December 15, 2012, at the Church of Christ in Hooker.  Inurnment will be in the Hooker Cemetery.  	    
Douglas Gene Grounds, son of the late Gene and Marge Grounds, was born February 25, 1953, at Liberal, Kansas, and passed away November 27, 2012, at his home in Wichita, Kansas, at the age of 59 years, 8 months, and 2 days.  	    
Doug grew up in Hooker and graduated from Hooker High School in 1971 and attended Southwestern Oklahoma State University.  He attended the Crosspoint Freewill Baptist Church in Wichita. He worked in the oilfield for many years for his father and also helped his brother at Pheasant Ridge Hunting in Hooker.  Doug enjoyed playing the guitar and harmonica and spending time with his family.  	    
Doug was preceded in death by his parents.  	    
He is survived by two brothers, Greg Grounds and wife, Teresa, and Chris Grounds and wife, Kelie, all of Hooker; one sister, Traci Williams of Alva; many nieces and nephews, great nieces and nephews, aunts, uncles, cousins, other relatives and friends.    
  Memorial contributions may be made to the Hooker Church of Christ Mission Fund or charity of your choice.
