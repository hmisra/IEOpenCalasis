Funeral services for Frances L. Sims, 80, will be 3:00 p.m. Saturday, July 15, 2006, at the Dacoma Church of God with Pastor Bob Brown and Rev. Earle Metcalf officiating.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Frances Louise Sims, daughter of the late John and Edrie (Hadley) Gatz, was born June 8, 1926, on the family farm near Avard, Oklahoma in Woods County, Oklahoma, and passed away July 13, 2006, at Integris Bass Baptist Health Center at the age of 80 years, 1 month and 5 days.  
Frances graduated from Avard High School and attended Northwestern State College.  On April 19, 1946, she was united in marriage to Thomas Henry Sims at Alva, Oklahoma.  They lived on their farm north of Avard where they farmed and ranched until moving to Alva in August of 1977.  She was an active member of the Dacoma Church of God.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Tom Sims, and one son, Sandy Lee Sims.  
Frances is survived by two sons, Johnny Sims and his wife, Debbie, of Amarillo, Texas, and Tommy Sims and his wife, Christi, of Weatherford, Oklahoma; one sister, Betty Murray of Topeka, Kansas, two daughters-in-law, Merla Sims of Enid, Oklahoma and Mona Sims of Weatherford, Oklahoma; twelve grandchildren, Todd Sims, Travis Sims and his wife, Karrin, Shawn Sims, Toby Sims and his wife, Stacy, Tyler Sims, Thad Sims, Shontue&amp; Anson and her husband, Clint, Meika Sims, Marque&amp; Canaan and her husband, Jesse, Toni Wilson and her husband, Randall, Heidi Knutson and her husband, Brad,  Brianna Sims; ten great grandchildren, Tae&amp;Lee Sims, Halah Canaan, Brayden Knutson, Bristen Knutson, Breven Knutson, Brock Sims, Darian Sims, Jaci Sims, Colton Sims, and Avery Sims; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Dacoma Church of God or Alva Church of God.
