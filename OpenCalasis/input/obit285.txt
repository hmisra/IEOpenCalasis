Funeral services for Julie Kaye Cade, of Cleo Springs, Oklahoma, will be 2:00 p.m. Sunday, January 6, 2008, at the United Methodist Church in Carmen, Oklahoma.  Pastor John Smith of the Alva Wesleyan Church will officiate and Rev. Lance Lenards will assist.  Burial will follow in Carmen City Cemetery under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC of Carmen, Oklahoma.   
Julie, daughter of Norma Marie Naugle and the late Lawrence Roy Naugle, was born April 10, 1971, at Enid, Oklahoma and, passed away Thursday, January, 3, 2008, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 36 years, 9 months and 23 days.  
She graduated from Carmen High School and later attended beauty school.  In December of 2004 she married Melvin Cade.  Julie worked as a waitress and sheet metal worker.  She worked the last few years at the Cleaning Closet. She had lived in Alva for many years before moving to Cleo Springs to live with her mother the past year. She was a member of the Alva Wesleyan Church. Julie enjoyed spending her time going to her kids ball games and other events.  
Julie was preceded in death by her father and an infant sister, Tammy Marie Naugle.  
She is survived by her husband, Melvin,  f Alva; her mother, Norma Naugle, of Cleo Springs; her children, LaTelle Graves, Henry Washington, and Henryetta Washington, all of Cleo Springs; her brother, Kyle Naugle and his wife, Brande, of Enid; her sisters, Shelly Miller of Alva, Traci Naugle of Oklahoma City, Patricia Sorrentino and her companion, Mickey Walker, of Fairview; nieces, nephews, other relatives and many friends.
