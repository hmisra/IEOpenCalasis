Funeral services for Ethelyn Virginia Nicholson, age 88, will be 2:00 p.m. Saturday, at the Waynoka Elm and Murrow Church of Christ with Darrell Edwards officiating.  Interment will be in the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka.  
Ethelyn Virginia Nicholson, daughter of the late Thomas Benton and Hazel Sarah (Davidson) Wilcox, was born July 21, 1918, northwest of Cleo Springs in Alfalfa County, Oklahoma, and passed away December 20, 2006 in Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 88 years, 4 months, and 29 days.  
Ethelyn attended the Elm Flat Rural School through the 8th grade and graduated from Cleo Springs High School.  On July 15, 1938, she was united in marriage to William Arthur Nicholson at Aline Christian Church.  She and Bill started farming east of Waynoka where they later bought a farm.  They lived on the farm until Bill retired when they moved to Waynoka.  She worked in two different grocery stores in Waynoka, Dr. McFarland?s Clinic, and retired after working several years at the First State Bank.    
She was a member of the Elm and Murrow Church of Christ.  She enjoyed sewing, but the most important things in her life were her family and her church.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Bill, on May 4, 2006; three brothers, Harry, Leonard, and Dan; three sisters, Ella Francis, Zirtha, and Emma.  
Ethelyn is survived by one son, Tommy Nicholson of Mooreland, Oklahoma; one brother, Alva Wilcox of Cleo Springs; one grandson; one granddaughter; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Elm and Murrow Church of Christ.  
Remembrances may be shared with the family at www.marshallfuneralhomes.com.
