Funeral services for Carolyn Jean Evans will be 10:30 a.m. Monday, August 23, 2010, at the Alva Church of God with Pastor Bob Brown officiating.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  There will be visitation with the family at the funeral home Sunday, August 22, 2010, from 5:00 p.m. to 7:00 p.m.  
Carolyn Jean Evans, daughter of Erlene Marie (Coulter) Evans and the late Henry Dean Evans, was born August 10, 1956, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away suddenly August 19, 2010, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 54 years and 9 days.  
Carolyn graduated from Alva High School.  She lived her entire life in Alva and enjoyed crafts, crocheting, embroidery, and gardening.  She was a member of the Alva Church of God and the VFW Ladies Auxiliary.  
She was preceded in death by her father, Dean Evans, on January 19, 2010.  
Carolyn is survived by her mother, Erlene Evans of Alva; and one brother, Melvin Evans and wife, Sherry, of Alva.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Alva Church of God Building Fund or the Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for heart research.
