Funeral services for Jacky Lynn Pitts will be 2:00 p.m. Wednesday, January 2, 2008, at the Wentworth Chapel with Rev. Max Ridgway officiating.  Interment will be at the Carmen City Cemetery under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC.  
Jacky Lynn Pitts, son of Betty (Harris) Pitts and the late Joseph Walter Pitts, was born February 28, 1961, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away December 29, 2007 at Integris Bass Baptist Hospital in Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 46 years, 10 months, and 1 day.  
Jacky attended school at Carmen and Cherokee.  He worked as an electrical journeyman.  He loved cars and antiques.  
Besides his father, he was preceded in death by his grandparents, Vern Harris and Ruby Harris Coyle.  
Jacky is survived by his mother, Betty, of Enid; daughters, Jacquolin Pitts, and Shasta Pitts, both of Kingfisher, and Hayley Pitts of Lahoma; one brother, Glenn Schlarb, Jr. Of Carmen; two sisters, Toni Starks and her husband, Richard, of Enid, and Sherry Summers of Fayetteville, North Carolina; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for Heart Research.
