Graveside service for Frank James Goff, will be at 2:00 p.m., Thursday, March 27, 2014, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery, with Rev. Bob Brown, officiating. Arrangements are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of&nbsp; Alva.  Frank Goff, son of the late Eli Edward and Minnie Ora (Jones) Goff, was born December 17, 1955, in Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away March 18, 2014, at the age of 58 years, 3 months, and 1 day.  Frank graduated from Seiling High School with the class of 1974. He lived in Waynoka for several years before moving to Alva to make his home. He loved to fish and was a handy man working with cattle and building fence. He worked with several people cutting iron into scrapes.  Frank married Wilma June Weese at Cherokee, Oklahoma.  He was preceded in death by his parents, a infant brother, Billy Lee; brother-in-law, Johnny Gatewood.  Frank is survived by his wife, Wilma of Alva; his brother, Dwaine Goff of Canyon City, Colorado, his sister, Virginia Gatewood of Alva; other relatives and friends.  Contributions may be made through the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.  &nbsp;
