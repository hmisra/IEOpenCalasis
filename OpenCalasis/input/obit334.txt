Funeral services for Johnny Q. Sittingdown, Jr. will be 2:00 p.m. Monday, March 31, 2014, at the Marshall Funeral Home Chapel with Rev. Barbara Fyffe officiating. Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  Johnny Q. Sittingdown, Jr., son of Odessa (Dougherty) Sittingdown and the late Johnny Q. Sittingdown, Sr. was born July 6, 1948, at Yale, Oklahoma, and passed away March 27, 2014, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 65 years, 8 months, and 11 days.  Johnny attended several schools, including Blackwell, and later got his GED. He worked for several refineries and auto companies before moving to Alva in 1980. He worked for the City of Alva several years until his retirement. He then owned and operated Iraâ€™s Bar and later worked at Alva Monument Company.  On March 17, 2014, he was baptized into the Methodist Church. He enjoyed collecting and trading antiques, going to auctions, and buying and selling cars. He was well known for helping others at anytime without the need of recognition. He helped several people financially, physically, and emotionally all through his life.  Besides his father, he was preceded in death by his brother, Frankie Reynolds.  Johnny is survived by his daughter, Terri Bates Leeper and husband, Jay, of Alva; his mother, Odessa Dougherty of Garden City, Kansas, one sister, Johnanna Borden and husband, Matt, of Garden City, Kansas; two grandchildren, Kate and Kayla Leeper, both of Alva; his uncle, Milton Sittingdown; his special friends, Paula and Darian Carothers; other relatives and many friends.  Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for cancer or mesothelioma research.  &nbsp;  .
