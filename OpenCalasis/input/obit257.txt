Loretta Gail Oshel, daughter of Pearl Rose English and Frank Marion English, born May 4, 1922 in her grandparents? home in Waynoka, Oklahoma, died September 10, 2007 in her home of 55 years in Alva, Oklahoma.    
Funeral services will be held at 10:00 a.m. Friday, September 14, 2007 in the Marshall Funeral Home Chapel.  Sean Evans, minister of the First Christian Church of Alva, will be officiating and Judge Dean Linder will be delivering the eulogy.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of the Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  The casket will remain closed; however, friends are invited to pay their respects throughout the day Thursday as well as to attend funeral services on Friday.  
After graduating from Waynoka High School, Loretta Gail attended Northwestern State College and continued her studies at Wesley Nurses Training School in Wichita KS. She married Corporal Warren Herbert Oshel, US Army Air Corps in Enid, Oklahoma on January 20, 1943.  They built their home together on 13th street in Alva.    
A successful wife and mother, Loretta Gail pursued her personal career by working for over 50 years for a number of local veterinarians including committing many years to Ridgeview Veterinary Hospital with Dr. McKinley and Dr. Lohmann. Loretta Gail was a member of the First Christian Church.  She was also a proud member of the Society of the Daughters of the American Revolution, the American Legion Auxiliary, and the Sigma Phi Gamma Sorority.  She enjoyed spending time in her yard and growing roses. Loretta Gail is survived by her mother Pearl English Crain of Alva, her three sons: Dean, Ken, and David, her daughter Lore, eleven grandchildren, and ten great-grandchildren.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the NWOSU Foundation.
