Funeral services for Robert Lee "Bob? Wiechman will be 11:00 a.m. Saturday, November 17, 2012, at the Zion Lutheran Church with Pastor Aaron Wagner officiating.  Interment will be in the Zion Lutheran Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Robert Lee "Bob? Wiechman, son of the late Adolph and Clara (Wieters) Wiechman, was born July 28, 1934, at Barnes, Kansas, and passed away November 13, 2012, at Kiowa, Kansas, at the age of 78 years, 3 months, and 15 days.  
Bob attended school in Clay Center Kansas.  He was proud to serve in the National Guard.  On October 2, 1960, he was united in marriage to Nadine Garrett at Derby, Kansas.  He farmed and ranched, worked as a carpenter, and was well known as a jack of all trades.  
He was baptized and confirmed into the Lutheran faith at the St. Paul Lutheran Church at Clay Center, Kansas, and was currently a member of the Alva Zion Lutheran Church.  He enjoyed woodworking and making crafts and toys for his children.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by his wife, Nadine, in 2010; and three brothers, Raymond, Elmer, and Wilbert Wiechman.  
Bob is survived by three sons, Myron Wiechman and his wife, Jerri, of Manchester, Oklahoma, Barry Wiechman and his wife, Vanita, of Winfield, Kansas, and Thomas Wiechman and his wife, Lisa, of Rogers, Arkansas; one daughter, Roberta Fischer of Kiowa, Kansas; one brother, Irvin Wiechman and wife, Wilma, of Valley Center, Kansas; and two sisters, Esther Reisen of Tucson, Arizona, and Ila Rinehardt, of Fort Worth, Texas.  
Also surviving are six grandchildren, Josh Wiechman of Wichita, Kansas, Jessica Wiechman, of Cherokee, Oklahoma, Wesley Wiechman of Rogers, Arkansas, Hannah Wiechman of Rogers, Arkansas, Brayden Wiechman of Rogers, Arkansas and Richard Fischer of Kiowa, Kansas; several great grandchildren, other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Zion Lutheran Church.
