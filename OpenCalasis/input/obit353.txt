There will be a memorial service for Charles Brasfield at 10:00 a.m., Saturday, August 16, 2014, at the Dacoma Church of God. He was cremated at his request and cremation arrangements are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.     
Charles Rickey Brasfield was born November 22, 1951, at Cullman, Alabama, to Charles William and Valera Faye (McKwon) Brasfield, and passed away at his home in Dacoma on August 12, 2014, at the age of 62 years, 8 months, and 20 days.    
He served in the United States Army in Vietnam as a helicopter gunner. Charles was united in marriage on April 1, 1994, to Cynthia Reubin at Clarksville, Arkansas.    
Charles worked for many years at Tyson foods. He and Cynthia have lived the past 8 years at Dacoma since moving from Dardanelle, Arkansas.    
Charles is preceded in death by his parents.    
He is survived by his wife, Cynthia, of Dacoma; a stepson, Jason Middleton, of Dacoma; a stepdaughter, Debbie Middleton, of Woodward; a sister, Margaret Clark and husband, Ray, of Altus, Arkansas; six grandchildren, Logan Powers of Oklahoma City, Katie McKinney of Dacoma, Dillon Middleton of Oklahoma City, McKayla Bittle of Heartland, Arkansas, and Margaret and Abigail Middleton, both of Dacoma; other relatives and friends.
