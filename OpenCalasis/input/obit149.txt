Graveside funeral services for Twyla Vinson, will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, April 27, 2012, at the Booker Cemetery, Davenport, Oklahoma. Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva. She may be viewed at the funeral home until Friday morning.   
Twyla Rochelle Vinson, daughter of Otis Mark Thomas and the late Annette (Moore) Thomas, was born October 27, 1975, in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, and passed away April 23, 2012, at Alva at the age of 36 years, 5 months, and 26 days.  
Twyla graduated from Davenport High School and attended Oklahoma State University. She attended the Living God Church. Twyla was united in marriage to Sean Vinson and they had two sons, Marquis and Tarek. She moved to Alva four years ago and worked at Beadles Nursing Home as a nurse aid. Before moving to Alva, she lived in Oklahoma City. Twyla loved music, dancing, and watching scary movies.  
Twyla was preceded in death by her mother.  
Surviving her are two sons, Marquis and Tarek Vinson of Tulsa, her father, Otis Thomas of Oklahoma City, several aunts, uncles, cousins, and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home for funeral expenses.
