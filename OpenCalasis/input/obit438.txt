Funeral services for Ruth Love will be 10:00 a.m. Friday, August 10, 2007 at the First Baptist Church in Alva with Dr. Greg Hook officiating.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Ruth Florence Love, the daughter of the late Lawrence and Myrtle Estella (Johnson) Callies was born April 30, 1935, in Norfolk, Nebraska, and passed away August 7, 2007, at St. Mary&amp;s Regional Medical Center in Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 72 years, 3 months, and 8 days.  
Ruth graduated from the Newman Grove High School in Newman Grove, Nebraska, with the Class of 1953.  She worked in the office at a creamery and then as a cook at the hospital in Newman Grove.  While she lived in Topeka, she helped care for the elderly in their homes.  
On April 30, 1977, she was united in marriage to Robert Lee Love in Topeka, Kansas.  They moved to Alva, Oklahoma in 1978.  
Ruth was a member of the First Baptist Church.  
She was preceded in death by her parents and two brothers, Rodney and Duane Callies.  
She is survived by her husband, Robert, of the home and a number of wonderful relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to First Baptist Church.
