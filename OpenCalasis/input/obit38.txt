Graveside services for Helen T. Veley were held 10:00 a.m. Tuesday, May 5, 2015, at the Carmen City Cemetery with Ron Kephart officiating. Arrangements were under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC.  	    
Helen Theresa Veley was born April 10, 1928, to the late Charles and Catherine (Doyle) Jones, in New York, and passed away on May 3, 2015, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 87 years and 23 days.  	    
Helen grew up and went to school in New York and New Jersey. She was united in marriage to Basil Franklin Veley on November 10, 1947, at Long Branch, New Jersey. After their marriage, they moved to Carmen and then Helen made Alva her home for the past 40 years. She was a member of the Catholic Church. Collecting antiques and especially antique dolls was one of her favorite hobbies. She enjoyed visiting with people.  	    
Besides her parents and her husband, she is preceded in death by three brothers, Billy, Charles, and Bobby Jones; and one sister, Margaret Howe.  	    
Helen is survived by two sons, Buddy Veley and Billy Veley, both of Carmen; one daughter, Maureen Gregory of Nescatunga; three grandchildren, Vanessa Boeckman and her husband, Justin, of Edmond, David Gregory of Fairview, and Andrea Veley of Alva; four brothers, Kenneth Jones of Jupiter Beach, Florida, Eddy Jones and his wife, Judy, of Eatontown, New Jersey, Tommy Jones of Eatontown, New Jersey, and Alvin Jones and his wife, Peggy, of Freehold, New Jersey; former daughter-in-law, Angela Veley, of Alva; other relatives and friends.  	    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral to the charity of your choice.
