Graveside services for Iva Jean Rose will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, November 20, 2012, at the Alva Municipal with Rev. Ron Pingelton and David Hamil officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.   
Iva Jean Rose, daughter of the late Albert Nathan and Revenna Luella (Arndt) Rose, was born July 3, 1932, on the farm near Cora, and passed away November 15, 2012, at Bartlesville, Oklahoma, at the age of 80 years, 4 months, and 12 days.  
Jean attended Farry School until her family moved to Alva where she graduated from Alva High School in 1951.  Upon finishing school, she went to work for Southwestern Bell Telephone Company at the Alva office.  Being offered promotions, she moved to the Enid office, then later to Oklahoma City where she retired after some 44 years with the company.  
After retiring, she lived in Oklahoma City, then moving back to Enid where she enjoyed dining with retired Southwest Bell employees.  She later moved to Caney, Kansas to be closer to family members.  
She enjoyed bowling with friends and reading a good book.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by an infant sister, Alma Viola Rose; her brother, Albert L. "Bud? Rose; and a sister, Gladys Ellen Rose.  
Jean is survived by four very important nephews in her life,  Leonard Max Rose and his wife, Mary, of Alva, DeWayne Lee Rose and his wife, Kathy, of Caney, Kansas, Lester Gene Rose and his wife, Nancy, of Wichita, Kansas, and Keith Allen Rose and his wife, Brenda, of Alva; several great nieces and nephews and great great nieces and nephews; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation.
