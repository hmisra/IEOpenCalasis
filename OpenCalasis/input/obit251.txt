A graveside memorial service for Monty Wimmer will be 2:00 p.m. Thursday March 19, 2009, at the Alva Municipal Cemetery. Arrangements are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Monty Blaine Wimmer, son of the late Henry Blaine and Ruby Elma (Wells) Rose Wimmer was born November 6, 1960, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away March 15, 2009, at Alva at the age of 48 years, 4 months, and 9 Days.  
Monty attended Alva School and later attained his G.E.D.  As a young child he was baptized into the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day  Saints. He worked different jobs as a general laborer.  He enjoyed art, stone carving, and collecting old knives.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by one sister, Marna Finney.  Monty is survived by two sisters, Afton Nichols and Elma McMurphy and her husband, Bill, all of Alva; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Oklahoma Medical Research Foundation for heart research.
