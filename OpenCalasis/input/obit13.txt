Funeral services for Rose Kost will be 10:30 a.m. Wednesday, February 16, 2011, at the Carmen Christian Church with Rev. Kirk Board officiating. Interment will be in the Carmen City Cemetery under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC, Carmen.  
Rose Marie (Church) Kost, daughter of the late Glenn Charles and Juana Ethel (Duel) Church, was born on November 6, 1936, at Cherokee, Oklahoma, and passed away on February 13, 2011, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 74 years, 3 months, and 7 days.  
Rose grew up and attended school in Carmen graduating with the class of 1954. She went to beauty school and opened her own shop in Carmen.  On July 31,1958, she was united in marriage to Richard Kost at Carmen, Oklahoma. To this union two children were born: Beth Ann and Kelly Lee.  They made their home in Carmen for several years before moving to Enid where they have lived the past 45 years.   
She was preceded in death by her parents.  
Rose is survived by her husband, Richard, of Enid; daughter, Beth Ann Watkins and husband, Tracy, of Weatherford; son, Kelly Kost and wife, Pam, of Foss; two grandsons, Talon Brady Watkins and Baylor Austin Watkins of Weatherford; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Hospice Circle of Love or the Diabetes Foundation.
