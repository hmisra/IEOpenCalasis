Graveside services for Leona Mae Corbin will be 3:00 p.m. Saturday, March 14, 2015, at Short Springs Cemetery with Pastor Bob Brown officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Leona Mae Corbin, daughter of the late Thomas Jay and Floy (Sanders) Palmer, was born November 13, 1930, at Ashley, and passed away March 10, 2015, at Alva, at the age of 84.    
Leona graduated from Cherokee High School with the Class of 1948.  She then worked at Monford's and TG&amp;Y.  Leona married Raymond Walter Corbin in 1952 and they moved to Las Vegas.  In 1981, they moved back to Alva where she went to work for Share Medical Center where she worked for 20 years.  Her manager, Ruth Cayot, was her best friend.  	    
Leona was a member of the Alva Church of God and the Red Hat Society.  She enjoyed making quilts, cooking, gardening.  She loved her two dogs, Beau and Pebbles.Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Raymond; two infant sisters; and a brother, Ernest J. Palmer.    
  Leona is survived by her son, Bobby Corbin and wife, Loretta, of Texas; her daughter, Teresa Parish of Bastrop, Texas; her sister, Barbara Case of Alva; her brother, Clinton Palmer of Kansas; five grandchildren; six great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.    
  Memorial contributions may made through the funeral home to the Alva Church of God.
