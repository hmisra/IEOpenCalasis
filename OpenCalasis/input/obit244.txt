Funeral services for Shala Jane Isabelle Washington will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, December 21, 2012, at the Hopeton Wesleyan Church with Pastor Marion Hadsell and Rev. Bob Brown officiating.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
Shala Jane Isabelle Washington, daughter of Jeffrey Lynn and Tammy Isabelle (Barton) Stacey, was born July 27, 1987, at El Dorado, Kansas, and passed away December 18, 2012, at Oklahoma City, Oklahoma, at the age of 25 years, 4 months, and 21 days.    
Shala graduated from Burlington High School.  On March 17, 2007, she was united in marriage to Dylan Andrew Washington at the Runneymede in Alva.  She lacked one semester in completing her Nursing Degree at Northwestern Oklahoma State University.  She worked at Share Convalescent Home as a CMA and the the Lite-N-Nite Convenience Store.    
Shala was preceded in death by her grandfather, James Stacey; her grandparents-in-law, Kenneth and Joy Davidson; and an infant brother-in-law, Daniel Washington.    
Shala is survived by her husband, Dylan, of Alva; her two daughters, Kori and Peyton Washington of Alva; her parents, Jeff and Chris Stacey of Sapulpa, and Tammy Stacey of Shakopee, Minnesota; two brothers, Cory Stacey and wife, Nikki, of Enid, and Caleb Stacey and wife, Ashley, of Alva; two brothers-in-law, Derek Washington of Enid, and Thomas Washington and wife, Jana, of Alva; her grandparents, Carolee Stacy of Burlington, Avis and Kirk Johnson of Kiowa, and Earl “Buddy” Barton of Alva; her mother and father-in-law, David and Marietta Washington of Alva; other relatives and many friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to establish an education fund for Kori and Peyton.
