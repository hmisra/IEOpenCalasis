Funeral services for Eunice Ellen Hinderliter will be 2:00 p.m. Friday, April 3, 2009, at the Waynoka Church of God with Pastor Ken Higgins officiating.  Interment will be in the Memorial Hill Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Eunice Ellen Hinderliter, daughter of the late Vernie Calvin and Hattie (Lam) McNeil, was born July 18, 1912, at May, Oklahoma, and passed away March 30, 2009, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 96 years, 8 months, and 12 days.  
Eunice graduated from Waynoka High School.  On September 3, 1932, she was united in marriage to Lester Hinderliter at Buffalo, Oklahoma.  She served as Activities Director at the Waynoka Nursing Center for a number of years.  
She was a member of the Waynoka Church of God, where she taught Sunday School and enjoyed singing in the choir.  She enjoyed ceramics painting, sewing, and fishing.  They enjoyed spending summers in Southfork, Colorado, which they did for 18 years.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by her husband, Lester, on April 18, 1994.  
Eunice is survived by one son, Veston Hinderliter and wife, Barbara, of Amarillo; one sister, Coleen Guyer and husband, Charles, of Amarillo; four grandchildren, Julie Glass and husband, Bob, of Waynoka, Kevin Hinderliter and wife, Connie, of Waynoka, Debbie McCall and husband, Doug, of Colorado Springs, Colorado, and Clayton Rose and wife, Julie, of Amarillo; 11 great grandchildren; 4 great great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Church of God Missions.
