Funeral services for Janie Coburn will be 1:30 p.m. Monday, December 8, 2014, at the Carmen United Methodist Church with Rev. John Bizzell officiating. Interment will follow in the Carmen City Cemetery under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC.  	    
Janie Beatrice Coburn, daughter of the late William Elbert and Ruby (Bowden) Robbins was born November 10, 1934, at Hemphill, Texas, and passed away December 3, 2014, at Enid, Oklahoma, at the age of 80 years and 23 days.  	    
Janie grew up in Jasper, Texas. She was united in marriage to Gemes Ivy Brown at Jena, Louisiana. To this union three sons were born: James, Carl, and John. Later she married John Tillman Coburn. She worked many years in a refinery.  	    
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by one grandson and two brothers, William “Joe” Robbins and James Robbins.  	    
Janie is survived by three sons, James Brown and wife, Betty, of Jasper, Texas, Carl Brown and wife, Sandra, of Jasper, Texas, and John Brown and girlfriend, Debora Bebee, of Carmen; 7 grandchildren, 16 great grandchildren, other relatives and friends.  	    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
