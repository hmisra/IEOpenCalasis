Graveside services for Devere E. Zook, age 70, will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, December 12, 2006, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Mike Marsalis, pastor of the Northside Church of Christ officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Devere, son of the late Harry Lester and Nettie Irene (McCully) Zook, was born November 20, 1936, southeast of Waynoka in Woods County, Oklahoma, and passed away December 11, 2006, at the Waynoka Nursing Center in Waynoka, Oklahoma, at the age of 70 years and 21 days.  
Devere attended Walnut Grove Schools.  He helped on the family farm.  
In June, 2002, he was baptized in the First United Methodist Church in Waynoka.  He enjoyed visiting and watching the high school football team play and practice.  
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by two brothers, Harry Zook and Francis Zook; and one sister, Lois Ralston.  
Devere is survived by one brother, Ivan Zook of Waynoka; one sister Linda Olson and her husband, Galen, of Amorita; one brother-in-law, Nelson Ralston of Avard; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka Nursing Center.
