Funeral services for James Leroy “Jim” Dunlap will be 10:00 a.m. Friday, June 27, 2014, at the Alva First Christian Church with Pastor Ron Pingelton officiating.  Interment will be 1:00 p.m. Friday at the Wakita Cemetery with Tyson Yarnell officiating under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.    
James Leroy “Jim” Dunlap, son of the late Clayton Harvey and Minnie Mary (McMurphy) Dunlap, was born May 21, 1941, at Alva, Oklahoma, and passed away June 23, 2014, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 73 years, 1 month, and two days.    
Jim graduated from Freedom High School and was trained to be a butcher.  He was united in marriage to Peggy Jean Hastings.  He worked as a handyman for anybody who needed anything done.    
He was a member of the Alva First Christian Church.  He was an excellent woodworker and enjoyed making picture frames, rocking horses, and signs.  He loved to fish and enjoyed playing dominoes and Solitaire.    
Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by his wife, Peggy, and one sister, Frieda Henson.    
Jim is survived by one son, David Kenyon and wife, Vida, of Alva; two daughters, Connie White-Buckley and husband, Robert, of Las Vegas, Nevada, and Jackie McBride and husband, George, of Thornton, Texas; two brothers, Howard Dunlap and wife, Sharyn, of Longdale, Oklahoma, and Harvey Dunlap and wife, Wendy, of Sharon, Oklahoma; two sisters, Patsy Jean Rogers of Coldwater, Kansas, and Catherine Bilyeu and husband, Darrel of Coldwater, Kansas; 13 grandchildren; 22 great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.    
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to St. Jude Children’s Hospital.
