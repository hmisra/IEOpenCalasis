Graveside services for Leota Decreta "Dody? Wood will be 10:00 a.m. Saturday, November 3, 2012, at the Waynoka Municipal Cemetery with Pastor Ken Higgins officiating.  Interment is under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Leota Decreta "Dody? Wood, daughter of the late Chester Arther and Eva Elmyra (Bartlett) Rawson, was born February 15, 1922, at Waynoka, Oklahoma, and passed away November 1, 2012, at Clovis, New Mexico, at the age of 90 years, 8 months, and 16 days.  
Dody attended Waynoka Schools.  On July 24, 1936, she was united in marriage to Garnett M. Bails at Waynoka, Oklahoma.  She was a housekeeper at Alva General Hospital and caretaker of her parents.  
She attended the Faith Christian Family Church in Clovis, New Mexico.  As a devoted family woman, she enjoyed her children and grandchildren.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by one son, Wayne Bails; one brother, Clifford Rawson; and one sister, Leona Sims.  
Dody is survived by one son, Robert Bails of Clovis, New Mexico; one brother, Donald Rawson of Alva; three grandchildren, Cynthia Carthel and husband, Michael, of Melrose, New Mexico, Tony Bails and wife, Vicky, of Bushland, Texas, and Lorrie Bails of Enid; six great grandchildren; one great great grandchild; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the American Heart Association.
