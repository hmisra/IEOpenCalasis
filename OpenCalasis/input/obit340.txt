Funeral services for Gregory Hall Robinson will be 2:00 p.m. Thursday, May 27, 2010, at the Carmen Christian Church with Rev. Kirk Board and Rev. John Bizzell officiating.  Interment will be at the Pleasant Ridge Cemetery near Aline, Oklahoma, under the direction of Wentworth Mortuary, LLC.  
Gregory Hall Robinson was born on August 22, 1977, at Enid, Oklahoma, and passed away May 24, 2010, at Enid, at the age of 32 years, 8 months, and 2 days.  
Gregory was raised in Carmen, Oklahoma, and graduated with the last graduating class at Carmen-Dacoma High School. He was a member of the Carmen Christian Church. After working for Perry Tire in Alva for a couple of years he began working on a harvest crew for several years. Gregory had a talent for drawing and enjoyed fishing.  
Gregory was preceded in death by his grandparents, Richard and Jenice Robinson, a brother and sister, Cody Bykerk and Taylee Murray.  
Surviving are his parents, Shelly and Randy Robinson of Carmen, and Gregory Alan and De Anna Murray of Aline; his brother, Kory Holt of Longdale; three sisters, Christa Boren and husband, Dale, of Norman, Brittney Bykerk of Skiatook, and Tasha Garoutte of Checotah; grandparents, Glenda and Eldon Beecher of Carmen, and Larry and Evona Murray of Aline; nephews, Aidyn Allan and Randy Hudsyn Boren of Norman; nieces, Gabrielle Taylor and Tailey Holt of Longdale;  numerous aunts and uncles, cousins, other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to help with funeral expenses.
