Memorial services for Mary Margaret "Maggie? Stapleton will be 11:00 a.m. Friday, June 11, 2010, at the Bible Baptist Church with Pastor John Clapp officiating.  She was cremated at her request with Marshall Funeral Home of Alva in charge of the cremation.  
Mary Margaret "Maggie? Stapleton, daughter of the late Richard Paul and Leone Hazel (Pagen) Jennings, was born February 20, 1937 at Salina, Kansas, and passed away June 5, 2010, at the age of 73 years, 3 months, and 16 days.  
Maggie enjoyed sewing, crocheting, and she loved cooking, especially her homemade bread and Christmas candy.  
Besides her parents, she was preceded in death by one sister, Doris Johnson.  
Maggie is survived by four daughters, Kelly Berger of Oklahoma City, Nikki Salisbury of Augusta, Kansas, Casey Canaan and husband, Willie, of Alva, and Miechelle Stapleton of Denver, Colorado; two brothers, Kenneth Jennings of Hutchison, Kansas, and Roger Jennings and wife, Vicki, of California; one sister, Shirley Towse and husband, Lyle, of Ponca City; 11 grandchildren; 14 great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made to the Servant Living Center Activity Fund, 616 S. Front Street, Medford, Oklahoma 73759.
