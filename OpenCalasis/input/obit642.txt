Funeral services for Marie Whitehead will be 10:00 a.m. Monday, April 21, 2008, at the Waynoka Church of God with Pastor John Brint officiating.  At her request, she will be cremated after the service.  The arrangements are under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Marie Whitehead, daughter of the late Sidney and Ora (Riggs) Pelfrey, was born March 12, 1916, at Sidney, West Virginia, and passed away April 19, 2008, at Alva, Oklahoma, at the age of 92 years, 1 month, and 7 days.  
Marie attended school in Wayne County, West Virginia.  On July 8, 1934, she was united in marriage to Sidney H. Kelly at Round Bottom, West Virginia.  He preceded her in death.  She worked as a cook and in nursing homes..  On September 25, 1978, she was united in marriage to Lester L. Whitehead at Merced, California.  He preceded her in death.  She had lived in Waynoka since 1997.  
She attended the Assembly of God Church in Waynoka.  A devout Christian, she was an ordained Church of God minister, taught Sunday School, and did a lot of missionary work. She attended Bro. John Brint&amp;s meetings at the nursing home.  
Besides her parents and her husbands, she was preceded in death by one son, Dan Ray Kelly in 2007; one daughter, Edith Pixler in 1983; three brothers, Chester Pelfrey, Donald as a young child, and James, who was killed white serving in the United States Air Force during World War II; and one sister, Dorothy McCellian.  
Marie is survived by three daughters, Ernestine Pixler and her husband, Alvin, of Waynoka, Violet Vourkais of Chow Chilla, California, and Sylvia Katie Kelly and her husband, Chuck Baldwin, of Arley, Alabama; one brother, Walter Pelfrey of Loma Linda, California; one sister, Edith Williams of Magnolia, Mississippi; 13 grandchildren; 18 great grandchildren; and 20 great great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka Nursing Home Activity Fund.
