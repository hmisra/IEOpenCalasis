Funeral services for Ruby Alice Perks, 83, will be 2:00 p.m. Tuesday, October 10,  2006, at Marshall Funeral Home Chapel.  Interment will be in the Alva Municipal Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.  
Ruby, daughter of the late Melvin and Martha (Hacker) Harding, was born July 29, 1923, in Woods County, Oklahoma, and passed away October 6, 2006, at her home at the age of 83 years, 2 months, and 7 days.  
She attended school in Alva.  On August 4, 1944, she was united in marriage to Norman Silvanis Perks at Dodge City, Kansas.  They  lived most of their married life in the Alva area. To this union seven children were born: Al, Rick, Ray, Roy, Bobby Joe, Norma, and Sherry.  She cleaned houses and offices and a laundry mat for many years.  Ruby enjoyed gardening, watching the birds, and crafting. She also attended the Town and Country Christian Church.  
Besides her parents and her husband, Norman, she was preceded in death by one son, Bobby Joe; four brothers, Ernest, Noble, Freddy, and Earl; three sisters, Rosie, Opal, and Ruth; and one grandson, Nick.  
Ruby is survived by four sons; Al Perks and his wife, Linda, of Alva, Rick Perks and his wife, Debbie, of Alva, Ray Perks and his wife, Nici, of Chickasha, and Roy Perks and his wife, Darlene, of Alva; two daughters, Norma Swallow and her husband, Bud, of Elmore City, and Sherry Kirkpatrick and her husband, Danny of Guthrie.  
Also surviving are two brothers, Dean Harding and his wife, Janie, of Amarillo, Texas, and Leonard Harding and his wife, Verda, of Fritz, Texas; three sisters, Ada Cheslic of Alva, Betty Anderson of Pampa, Texas, and Ramona Muegge and her husband, Mike, of Lamont, Oklahoma; 16 grandchildren; 25 great grandchildren; other relatives and friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to help with the funeral expenses.
