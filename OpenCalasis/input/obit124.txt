Funeral services for Kenneth Stephen Siler will be 10:00 a.m. Tuesday, May 4, 2010, at the Waynoka School Multipurpose Building with Rev. Marvin Stuckey officiating.  Interment will be at 3:00 p.m. in the Tonkawa Cemetery at Tonkawa, Oklahoma, under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Waynoka, LLC.  
Kenneth Stephen Siler, son of the late Kenneth Elmer and Audrey Lois (Veal) Siler, was born June 24, 1950, at Ponca City, Oklahoma, and passed away April 30, 2010, at his home in Waynoka, Oklahoma, at the age of 59 years, 10 months and 6 days.  
After Kenneth graduated from Ponca City High School he attended Northwestern Oklahoma State University where he received his degree in Language Arts.  He then received his Master?s Degree in Speech Pathology from Oklahoma State University.  
He worked for a time as a radio station advertising salesman in Clovis, New Mexico.  He was involved in the Cooperative Education Project.  He taught at many area schools, including Cherokee, Aline-Cleo, Buffalo, Freedom, Taloga, and Waynoka.  On April 3, 1999, he was united in marriage to Katherine Heidlebaugh at Buffalo, Oklahoma.  
He was preceded in death by his parents.  
Kenneth is survived by his wife, Katherine; two sons, Darreld Gates and his wife, Nikki, of Yukon, Oklahoma; Danial Gates and his wife, Amie, of Laverne, Oklahoma; one brother, Scott Siler and his wife, Terri, of Newkirk, Oklahoma; two sisters, Terri Cleveland and her husband, Harold, of Florida and Leighann Buffard and her husband, Wayne, of Billings, Oklahoma.  
Also surviving are five grandchildren, Katie Blair, Laura Blair, Jonathan Siler Gates and Michelle Gates, all of Yukon, Oklahoma and Conner Gates of Laverne, Oklahoma; other relatives and many friends.  
Memorial contributions may be made through the funeral home to the Waynoka First United Methodist Church.
