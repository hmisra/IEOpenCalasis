ARTHUR FRANCIS RUSH, 78, of Alva, was born September 9, 1935, in Alva to Clarence Albert and Louella Mae (Cummins) Rush.  He passed away November 25, 2013, at his home in Alva.  He was cremated at his request and a family service will be held at a later date.  Besides his parents, he was preceded in death by four brothers.  He is survived by three children, Francis Rush, Debra Swanner, and Anita Schwake; five grandchildren, and seven great grandchildren.
