Funeral services for Josten Dane Heaton will be 1:00 p.m. Monday, April 4, 2011, at the Alva First United Methodist Church with Rev. Terry Martindale officiating.  Interment will be in the Capron Cemetery under the direction of Marshall Funeral Home of Alva.

Josten is survived by his parents, Jeff and Jaci Heaton; his brother, Jantz Dace Heaton; his grandparents, Dan and Karen Heaton of Alva, Jean Ann Moore and partner, Arlyn Norman, of Hopeton, and Tommy and Gloria Moore of Stratford; great grandparents, Julia Roberts of Alva, Joe D. Heaton of Alva, Norma Jean Jones of Thomas, and Robert and Jane Avery of Ada.  
In lieu of flowers, the family requests memorial contributions be made through the funeral home to the March of Dimes.
